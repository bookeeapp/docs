# Member Profiles

Member profiles are the foundation of your Kenko system. They store all essential information about your members, allowing you to provide personalized service and track member activities.

## Creating Member Profiles

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Member Management">
    Navigate to the **Members** section in your Kenko dashboard.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add New Member">
    Click the **+ New Member** button in the top right corner of the screen.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Basic Information">
    Complete the required fields:
    - First and last name
    - Email address
    - Phone number
    - Birthdate (optional)
    - Emergency contact (optional)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Membership Details">
    Select the appropriate membership type or package for this member from the dropdown menu. You can also:
    - Set a custom start date
    - Apply membership discounts
    - Set renewal preferences
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Upload Photo">
    Add a profile photo to help staff identify members (optional but recommended).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Profile">
    Click **Save** to create the member profile. The new member will receive a welcome email with account setup instructions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Member Profiles

### Editing Profiles

1. Locate the member using the search function
2. Click on their name to open their profile
3. Select the **Edit** button in any section you wish to modify
4. Make your changes and click **Save**

### Adding Custom Fields

You can customize member profiles to collect additional information specific to your business:

1. Go to **Admin Settings** > **Member Management** > **Custom Fields**
2. Click **+ Add Field**
3. Configure the field type (text, dropdown, checkbox, etc.)
4. Set visibility permissions (admin-only or member-visible)
5. Click **Save**

<Warning>
  Custom fields will be applied to all existing and new member profiles.
</Warning>

### Member Tags

Use tags to categorize and segment your members:

1. Open a member's profile
2. Click **+ Add Tag** in the Tags section
3. Select from existing tags or create a new one
4. Use tags for targeted marketing, reporting, or special access
