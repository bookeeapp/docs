# Deep Dive into managing contacts

Every contact in Kenko — whether a lead, trial user, or active member — has a rich profile that captures their relationship with your business.

The contact profile is designed to give you and  your team full visibility and control over each person's history, preferences, and engagement.

## Editing a Contact Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Profile">
    Use the global search bar or go to the **Contacts** tab to find the profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Edit">
    In the top-right corner of the profile, click **Edit** to modify fields like name, contact info, birthday, and more.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Review your updates and click **Save** to apply.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Merging Duplicate Contacts

<Steps>
  <Step title="Identify Duplicates">
    Search for similar names or email addresses in your **Contacts** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open One Profile">
    From the Actions menu, choose **Merge Profile**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Duplicate">
    Search and select the second contact to merge.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Merge">
    Review data from both profiles and confirm to merge them into one.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Final Tips

- Keep profiles up to date to avoid communication issues and improve service quality.
- Use tags, documents, and timeline views to give staff fast, actionable insights.
- Merge duplicates regularly to maintain clean, consolidated records.

## Managing Member Profiles

### Editing Profiles

1. Locate the member using the search function
2. Click on their name to open their profile
3. Select the **Edit** button in any section you wish to modify
4. Make your changes and click **Save**

### Adding Custom Fields

You can customize member profiles to collect additional information specific to your business:

1. Go to **Admin Settings** \> **Member Management** \> **Custom Fields**
2. Click **\+ Add Field**
3. Configure the field type (text, dropdown, checkbox, etc.)
4. Set visibility permissions (admin-only or member-visible)
5. Click **Save**

<Warning>
  Custom fields will be applied to all existing and new member profiles.
</Warning>

### Member Tags

Use tags to categorize and segment your members:

1. Open a member's profile
2. Click **\+ Add Tag** in the Tags section
3. Select from existing tags or create a new one
4. Use tags for targeted marketing, reporting, or special access
