# Manage Contacts in the CRM

Kenko’s **Contact Management** module is designed to offer fitness and wellness businesses a streamlined solution for managing both **Leads** and **Customers**. It helps organize contacts efficiently and track their journey through your services, ensuring smooth interactions and record-keeping.

## Key Capabilities

<CardGroup cols="2">
  <Card title="Customer Profiles" icon="user">
    Manage customer details like membership status, contact information, payment history, and service usage.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Leads Management" icon="users">
    Manage potential customers in the introductory stages like trial memberships or promo offers.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Lead Pipeline" icon="timeline">
    Track leads through different stages like New, Trial, Follow-up, and Interested.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Document Management" icon="file">
    Upload important documents like waivers, signed forms, or contracts to profiles.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Leads vs. Customers

Understanding the difference between **Leads** and **Customers** helps you optimize your efforts in nurturing and managing each contact type

<CardGroup cols="2">
  <Card title="Leads" icon="users">
    Leads are prospects in the **introductory offer** or **trial membership** phase. They have not yet made a purchase, and your goal is to convert them into paying customers through follow-ups and marketing campaigns.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Customers" icon="user-check">
    Customers have **active memberships** or **bookings**. They have made a purchase, signed up for memberships, or booked sessions. They are fully integrated into your business's operations.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Key Differences

**Leads**: Primarily for individuals who are in the trial phase or interested in the intro offer.

Lead Pipeline Management

Kenko’s **Lead Pipeline** enables efficient management and tracking of leads as they progress through various stages.

1. **New Leads**: Fresh prospects who have shown interest. Businesses can assign leads to team members for further nurturing.
2. **Follow-Up**: Leads that require follow-up actions to convert them into customers.
3. **Will Buy Later**: Leads that are interested but need more time before making a decision.

<CardGroup cols="2">
  <Card title="Lead Stages" icon="stairs">
    Track leads through the pipeline and move them between stages based on progress.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Follow-Up" icon="repeat">
    Manage and automate follow-ups to nurture leads and convert them to active customers.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  Additional Lead stages can be added according to business requirements from Settings \> Leads

## Customer Profile Components

The **Customer Profile** in Kenko CRM contains all the necessary information for managing customer interactions effectively. The key components of a customer profile include:

<CardGroup cols="2">
  <Card title="Basic Information" icon="id-card">
    Includes personal details such as name, email, phone number, address, and membership status.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Membership & Payments History" icon="credit-card">
    Displays active membership, payment history, membership expiration date, and due payments.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Related Contacts" icon="link">
    Easily add related contacts, such as family members or referrals, and link them to the customer profile.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Documents" icon="file">
    Upload important customer documents like signed waivers, contracts, or liability forms directly to the profile.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Booking History" icon="calendar-days">
    Booked sessions information including classes, appointments and course session.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Referral Information" icon="person-sign">
    Check who has been referred by your customer and what rewards they received.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Customer Segments

Kenko lets you organize and understand your audience by segmenting customers based on their Activity, Memberships, and Attributes. This helps tailor communication and improve engagement.

<CardGroup cols="2">
  <Card title="Contact Attributes" icon="user-tag">
    Customers details like name, contact info, birthday, gender and contact tags
  </Card>
  <Card title="Purchases" icon="money-bill">
    Customers who have made purchases, and various other payment attributes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Lifetime Activity" icon="user">
    Contact's activity information including Visits, Bookings and Cancellations.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Pricing Options" icon="coin-vertical">
    Members and their respective pricing options attributes.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

You can these segments to send targeted campaigns or as a part of <u>Journeys</u> ( Automated workflows ).

Additional Features

Kenko CRM also includes various features to streamline contact management:

- **Custom Filtering and Searching**: Quickly find customers or leads based on different criteria such as name, membership status, and more.
- **Bulk Actions**: Perform actions such as archiving, adding tags, or sending messages to multiple contacts at once.
