# Explore Kenko's Pricing Options

Kenko allows you to create a variety of membership types and pricing options, each with unique features that affect how contacts interact with your business. Below are the different types of membership pricing options and key membership features.

## Types of Pricing Options

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Subscriptions">
    A recurring payment plan where members pay on a set schedule (e.g., monthly). This option ensures ongoing access to services.

    - **How it affects the contact**: Provides automatic renewals, allowing for continuous access without manual intervention.
    - **What's Included:** Credits ( Limited or Unlimited every cycle ) that can be spent on Classes/Appointments.
    - **Perks**: Some perks can also include Buddy passes ( Free or Paid ), Free access to Videos by the studio.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Credit packs">
    A one-time purchase of credits that can be used to book classes, services, or products, often with a fixed validity period.

    - **How it affects the contact**: Credits are deducted as used, and members can access services on a pay-as-you-go basis.
    - **What's Included**: Fixed or Unlimited credits for a set duration
    - **Perks**: Flexible usage, no long-term commitment required.  Buddy passes ( Free or Paid ) and Free access to Videos by the studio.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Intro Offers">
    Special pricing options for new customers, often offering discounts for the first month or trial period to encourage sign-ups.

    - **How it affects the contact**: Attracts new users by offering a low-cost introduction to the service.
    - **Perks**: Significant discount on initial purchases, typically leading to longer-term membership conversion.
    - **Restrictions**: Studios can put a cap on the total number of allowed purchases of Intro-offers. This restriction can be Location/Region or Business level.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Top-up">
    This option allows members to add more credits to their account once they are nearing depletion, maintaining access to services.

    - **How it affects the contact**: Ensures continued service availability once the initial credits are used up.
    - **Perks**: Convenient for members who prefer flexibility over fixed-term commitments.
    - **Restrictions**: Top-ups have to be purchased in addition to a credit pack or a subscription. These cannot be used as a standalone Membership option.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Best Practices

<CardGroup cols="2">
  <Card title="Use Auto-Renew for Long-Term Engagement" icon="rotate">
    Enable auto-renew for subscriptions and credit packs to maintain member continuity and reduce churn.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Set Thoughtful Pause & Cancellation Policies" icon="pause">
    Define clear rules for pauses and cancellations, including minimum cycle commitments and cancellation fees.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Encourage 'Bring a Friend' Usage" icon="user-plus">
    Leverage Buddy Passes to grow leads organically—make them a default perk in select plans.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Enable Booking Ahead of Expiry" icon="calendar-check">
    Let members schedule sessions beyond the membership's end date to reduce last-minute booking pressure.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Apply Rollover Caps Smartly" icon="arrows-turn-right">
    Let unused credits roll over, but set a cap to prevent hoarding and encourage consistent attendance.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Use Intro Offers as Conversion Tools" icon="tag">
    Limit intro offer purchases and pair them with follow-up campaigns to convert leads into long-term members.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Utilize Payment Plans for High-Value Memberships" icon="money-bill-trend-up">
    Offer split payments for premium plans to lower entry barriers without compromising revenue.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Track and Manage Shared Memberships" icon="users">
    Actively monitor who shares memberships to ensure fair usage and accurate benefits distribution.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
