# Kenko Business App

The **Kenko Business App** is your on-the-go command center for studio operations. Designed for fitness and wellness staff, this mobile CRM lets instructors and front-desk teams manage clients, classes, check-ins, and daily tasks—all from their phones.

Whether you're on the studio floor, in transit, or covering the front desk, the Kenko Business App keeps your team efficient, informed, and always connected.

## Key Features

<CardGroup cols="2">
  <Card title="Real-Time Schedule Access" icon="calendar">
    View and manage upcoming classes and appointments, track bookings, waitlists, and cancellations—all updated in real-time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Client Check-In" icon="user-check">
    Easily check in clients as they arrive, ensuring accurate attendance records and streamlined front-desk operations.

  <Card title="Self-Serve Kiosk Mode" icon="tablet-screen-button">

## Getting Started

The Kenko Business App is available on both platforms:

- [Download on the App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/kenko-business/id1531705941)
- [Get it on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.studioyou.studioyoubusinessapp)

Once installed, staff can log in using their Kenko credentials. Admins can manage access and permissions via the Kenko CRM dashboard.

<Note>
  Ensure each team member is assigned the correct role to control access to features and client data.
</Note>
