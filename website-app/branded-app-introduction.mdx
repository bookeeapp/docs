## Setting Up Your Branded App

<Steps>
  <Step title="Initiate App Setup">
    Contact your Kenko onboarding specialist or navigate to **Admin Settings** \> **Website & App** \>  **Mobile App Setup** to begin the process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide Branding Assets">
    Upload required design elements

    - Business logo (high resolution)
    - Brand color palette
    - App icon design
    - Splash screen image
    - Featured images for app screens

    <Note>
      We provide design guidelines for each asset to ensure optimal quality on all devices.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure App Settings">
    Customize the app functionality

    - Features to enable/disable
    - Navigation menu structure
    - Default screens and views
    - Content organization
    - User permission levels
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create App Content">
    Develop essential content

    - Welcome messages
    - About us information
    - Instructor bios
    - Class descriptions
    - Policies and FAQ
    - Contact information
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and Approve">
    Our team will create your app based on your specifications

    1. Review the app preview
    2. Request any necessary adjustments
    3. Approve the final design and functionality
  </Step>
  <Step title="App Submission">
    Once approved, we handle the submission process

    - App Store (iOS) submission
    - Google Play Store (Android) submission
    - Addressing any platform requirements

    <Warning>
      App approval typically takes 1-2 weeks for initial submission. Plan accordingly for your launch timeline.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch and Promotion">
    After app approval

    - Receive download links and marketing materials
    - Announce to your members
    - Provide installation guidance
    - Monitor initial adoption
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Ongoing App Management

Keep your app fresh and engaging with regular updates

1. **Content Refreshes**: Update class descriptions, instructor profiles, and promotional content through your Kenko dashboard
2. **Feature Updates**: New Kenko features are automatically added to your app with platform updates
3. **Push Notifications**: Send timely communications to drive engagement
4. **Usage Analytics**: Monitor app adoption, feature usage, and booking patterns
5. **Member Feedback**: Collect and respond to app reviews and feature requests

<Warning>
  Major app design changes may require a new app submission and approval process. Minor content updates can be made instantly. Re-builds might incur additional fee.
</Warning>
