## Customer App Customization

The Kenko-powered **Customer App** (iOS \+ Android) is your branded mobile booking interface for members.

### Where to Configure

Navigate to `Settings ‚Üí Customer App`

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Service Availability" icon="mobile">
    Control which services appear to clients in the app

    - Toggle visibility for Classes, Appointments, Courses
    - Choose default landing tab
    - Hide services not currently in use

    ![Screenshot2025 05 09at12 06 22PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-05-09at12.06.22PM.png)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Info>
  These changes apply immediately and let you simplify the app UI for your clients.
</Info>

## Website Portal Menu Bar Customization

Your Website Portal is the logged-in web experience for members. You can modify the menu bar to show only relevant options.

### Where to Configure

Navigate to `Settings ‚Üí Website Portal`

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Navigation Tabs" icon="bars">
    Add or remove

    - Login
    - Class Schedule
    - Pricing
    - Appointments
    - Video on Demand

    You can also

    - Rearrange the order of tabs
    - Rename labels
    - Link to external pages (e.g. blog, FAQ)

    ![Screenshot2025 05 09at12 08 01PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-05-09at12.08.01PM.png)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
