# Introduction to Kenko's Website Booking Widget

Kenko's Website Booking Widget allows you to embed a fully functional booking interface directly into your website via an iFrame. This integration ensures a seamless and branded experience for your clients, enabling them to browse, book, and pay for classes or appointments without leaving your site.

## How to Embed the Widget

To add the Kenko booking widget to your website

1. **Access the Embed Code**: Log in to your Kenko dashboard and navigate to the 'Website Widget' section to retrieve your unique iFrame embed code.
2. **Insert into Your Website**: Paste the iFrame code into the HTML of the desired page on your website where you want the booking interface to appear.
3. **Customize as Needed**: Adjust the width, height, and styling within the iFrame code to match your website's design and layout.

<Note>
  For optimal performance and user experience, ensure that the page where the widget is embedded is responsive and does not have conflicting scripts or styles.
</Note>
