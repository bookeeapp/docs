## Team Communication

Keep your team connected and informed with our communication tools.

- **Team Chat**: Built-in messaging for quick team communication
- **Announcements**: Share important updates with your entire team
- **Task Assignment**: Assign and track tasks for team members
- **Document Sharing**: Share important documents and resources with your team

## Instructor App

Give your instructors the tools they need on the go with our dedicated instructor app.

- **Class Schedule**: View upcoming classes and client information
- **Client Notes**: Access and update client notes and progress
- **Check-In Management**: Manage class check-ins and attendance
- **Substitute Management**: Request and manage class substitutions

## Performance Tracking

Monitor and improve instructor performance with our tracking tools.

- **Client Feedback**: Collect and analyze client feedback for each instructor
- **Class Attendance**: Track attendance rates for different instructors
- **Retention Metrics**: Measure how well instructors retain clients
- **Goal Setting**: Set and track performance goals for your team

## Payroll Management

Simplify instructor compensation with our payroll management features.

- **Variable Pay Calculation**: Calculate pay based on class type, attendance, or flat rates
- **Commission Tracking**: Track and calculate sales commissions
- **Automated Reporting**: Generate payroll reports for easy processing
- **Direct Deposit Integration**: Connect with popular payroll providers

## Access Controls

Ensure data security with role-based access controls.

- **Custom Roles**: Create custom roles with specific permissions
- **Granular Permissions**: Control access to sensitive information
- **Activity Logging**: Track user actions for accountability
- **Secure Login**: Two-factor authentication and secure login options

## Training & Onboarding

Streamline the onboarding process for new team members.

- **Training Materials**: Store and share training resources
- **Certification Tracking**: Monitor instructor certifications and expirations
- **Onboarding Checklists**: Create standardized onboarding processes
- **Performance Reviews**: Schedule and track regular performance reviews
