# Business Dashboard

The Kenko Business Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of your fitness business's performance metrics in one centralized location. This guide will help you understand and leverage the dashboard to make informed decisions.

## Accessing Your Dashboard

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to Kenko">
    Visit [app.gokenko.com](https://app.gokenko.com) and sign in with your administrator credentials.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Navigate to Dashboard">
    The Business Dashboard is your default landing page after login. You can also access it anytime by clicking **Dashboard** in the left navigation menu.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select View">
    Choose your preferred dashboard view:
    - **Overview**: General business performance
    - **Financial**: Revenue and payment metrics
    - **Membership**: Member growth and retention
    - **Attendance**: Class and visit analytics
    - **Custom**: Your personalized dashboard layout
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Customizing Your Dashboard

Tailor the dashboard to highlight the metrics most important to your business:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Customization Mode">
    Click the **Customize** button in the top-right corner of the dashboard.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Widgets">
    Select from available widgets:
    - Click **+ Add Widget**
    - Browse the widget library
    - Select widgets to add to your dashboard
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Arrange Layout">
    Organize your dashboard:
    - Drag and drop widgets to reposition
    - Resize widgets using the corner handles
    - Create sections with the **Add Section** button
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Widgets">
    Customize each widget:
    - Click the settings icon on any widget
    - Set data ranges and parameters
    - Choose visualization style
    - Configure alerts and thresholds
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Layout">
    Click **Save Layout** to preserve your customizations.
    
    <Note>
      You can create multiple custom dashboard layouts for different purposes by clicking **Save As** and giving each layout a unique name.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Data Filtering and Analysis

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="filter" title="Applying Filters">
    Refine your dashboard data with these filtering options:
    
    - **Date Range**: Set custom time periods or use presets (today, this week, this month, etc.)
    - **Location**: Filter by specific location(s) for multi-location businesses
    - **Membership Type**: Focus on specific membership categories
    - **Class Type**: Analyze performance of particular class formats
    - **Demographics**: Filter by member attributes (age, gender, etc.)
    
    Access filters by clicking the **Filter** button at the top of the dashboard.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="search-plus" title="Drilling Down">
    Explore data in greater detail:
    
    1. Click any metric or chart on the dashboard
    2. Select **View Details** or click directly on a data point
    3. Access expanded analytics and breakdowns
    4. Apply secondary filters for deeper analysis
    5. Toggle between different visualization types
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="file-export" title="Exporting Data">
    Extract data for external analysis:
    
    - Click the export icon on any widget
    - Choose your preferred format (CSV, Excel, PDF)
    - Select data range and parameters
    - Download the file or send directly to email
    
    Schedule regular exports via **Admin Settings** > **Reports** > **Scheduled Exports**.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Setting Business Goals

Track progress against your business objectives:

1. Navigate to **Dashboard** > **Goals** > **Set Goals**
2. Select a metric category (revenue, membership, attendance, etc.)
3. Define your target value and timeframe
4. Optionally set milestone targets
5. Save your goals

Once set, goals appear as overlay indicators on relevant dashboard widgets, showing progress bars or distance-to-target metrics.

## Dashboard Sharing and Reports

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Scheduled Reports">
    Set up automated reports:
    1. Go to **Dashboard** > **Reports** > **Scheduled Reports**
    2. Click **+ New Scheduled Report**
    3. Select dashboard view or specific widgets to include
    4. Choose recipients and frequency
    5. Add optional comments or highlights
    6. Save and activate
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Generate On-Demand Reports">
    Create instant reports:
    1. Click **Export** on the dashboard
    2. Select content to include
    3. Choose format (PDF, interactive report, etc.)
    4. Add custom title and notes
    5. Generate and download or share
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Share Dashboard Access">
    Provide dashboard access to team members:
    1. Go to **Admin Settings** > **User Management**
    2. Select user or create new user account
    3. Under **Permissions**, enable **Dashboard Access**
    4. Set appropriate access level (view only, full access, etc.)
    5. Save changes
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Interpreting Dashboard Insights

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="Revenue Analysis" icon="chart-pie">
    **What to look for**: Track which revenue streams are growing or declining. Monitor recurring vs. one-time revenue sources. Identify days or weeks with unusual patterns.
    
    **Action items**: Adjust pricing for underperforming offerings. Promote high-margin services. Create targeted campaigns for slow periods.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Member Engagement" icon="hand-pointer">
    **What to look for**: Identify visit frequency patterns. Monitor booking habits. Track which members are increasing or decreasing their engagement.
    
    **Action items**: Create re-engagement campaigns for declining visit patterns. Offer incentives during typical drop-off points in the member lifecycle.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Class Performance" icon="dumbbell">
    **What to look for**: Compare attendance rates across class types, instructors, and time slots. Identify consistently under-booked or waitlisted classes.
    
    **Action items**: Adjust schedule to maximize popular class times. Consider replacing or retraining for consistently underperforming classes.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
