Kenko offers a curated collection of **Journey Templates** that help you automate essential customer communications without starting from scratch. These templates are optimized for driving engagement, reviews, retention, and revenue.

## How to Get Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Journeys">
    Go to `Marketing > Journeys` and click `Create Journey`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a Template">
    Select a ready-made template based on your current marketing goal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize">
    Edit blocks, emails, wait times, and rules if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch">
    Publish your journey and start driving results\!
  </Step>
</Steps>
