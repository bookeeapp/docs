# Introduction to Journeys

Kenko Journeys is a powerful marketing automation engine built specifically for fitness and wellness businesses. It helps you design customized, automated workflows that engage, convert, and retain your clients — all with minimal manual intervention.

## Why Kenko Journeys is Unique

Unlike other automation systems, Kenko Journeys is:

- **Built for Fitness Studios**: Pre-configured with fitness-specific triggers, actions, and metrics.
- **Fully Visual**: Drag-and-drop builder that's easy to use even for non-technical users.
- **Omni-Channel**: Supports email, SMS, push notifications, and app messaging.
- **Deeply Integrated**: Tied directly into your CRM, Memberships, Appointments, and Classes for real-time automation.
- **Result-Driven**: Studios using Journeys have seen up to **30% increase** in client retention and **25% boost** in revenue within months.
