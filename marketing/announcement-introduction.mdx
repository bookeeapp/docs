# Introduction to Announcements

Kenko Announcements provide a powerful way to communicate directly with your customers through in-app messaging. Whether it's a quick alert, a detailed update, or a promotional push, announcements ensure that your studio's voice reaches clients in real-time — directly on their mobile app or web interface.

## Use Cases

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Operational Updates" icon="info">
    Notify customers about class cancellations, new schedules, holiday closures, or any on-the-go change. Example: “Class cancelled due to rain.”
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="New Launches & Offers" icon="tag">
    Promote new class packs, upcoming workshops, limited-time offers, or newly opened branches. Keep users engaged with fresh opportunities.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Feature or App Announcements" icon="mobile">
    Inform users about updates to your branded app, new booking features, or referral programs to drive usage and adoption.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Reactivation & Engagement" icon="bell">
    Re-engage inactive users by highlighting benefits they’re missing out on or sending reminders with incentives.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Types of Announcements

Kenko offers three flexible formats to suit different communication needs:

| Type                  | Description                                                     | Ideal For                                          |
| --------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| **Detailed Cards**    | In-app cards with a title, image, description, and optional CTA | Promotional content, feature updates, newsletters  |
| **Banner**            | Small, dismissible banner at the top of the app                 | Quick alerts like closures, weather disruptions    |
| **Push Notification** | Direct push to a user's device                                  | High-priority messages needing immediate attention |

<info>
  You can preview how each announcement appears on both mobile and web before publishing.
</info>

## Benefits of Announcements

<CardGroup cols="2">
  <Card title="Instant Visibility" icon="eye">
    Announcements appear directly in the app, ensuring maximum visibility without needing email or SMS.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Increased Engagement" icon="chart-line">
    Banners and cards link directly to app actions or external pages, enabling instant follow-through.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Real-Time Updates" icon="clock">
    Instantly update users on location-level changes with no dependency on external communication channels.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Performance Tracking" icon="chart-bar">
    Track impressions and clicks to understand what resonates with your audience.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<info>
  Announcements can be targeted per location and scheduled with start/end dates. This ensures maximum relevance and minimal clutter.
</info>

## When to Use Announcements

| Scenario                      | Announcement Type          |
| ----------------------------- | -------------------------- |
| Urgent class cancellation     | Banner / Push Notification |
| Launching a new app feature   | Detailed Card              |
| Seasonal offer or discount    | Detailed Card              |
| Re-engaging inactive clients  | Push Notification          |
| Promoting a limited-time pack | Card with CTA              |
