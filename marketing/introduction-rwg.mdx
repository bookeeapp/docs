# Reserve with Google (RwG)

**Reserve with Google** is a seamless integration between your Kenko-powered booking system and Google Search, Maps, and Assistant. It enables potential clients to discover, book, and pay for your services — right from Google.

Whether someone searches for "yoga class near me" or finds your studio on Google Maps, RwG allows them to view availability and reserve a spot instantly.

## How It Works for Clients

Customers searching for services like yoga, pilates, or training sessions on Google will:

- See your studio listed in the search results with a **“BOOK ONLINE”** button.
- Be redirected to your live schedule powered by Kenko.
- View individual classes or services with direct **“BOOK”** buttons.
- Select their desired session and complete the booking — all within the Google interface.

<Info>
  They never have to visit your website, call your studio, or download an app. It’s frictionless and fast.
</Info>
