# Reserve with Google (RwG)

**Reserve with Google** is a seamless integration between your Kenko-powered booking system and Google Search, Maps, and Assistant. It enables potential clients to discover, book, and pay for your services — right from Google.

Whether someone searches for "yoga class near me" or finds your studio on Google Maps, RwG allows them to view availability and reserve a spot instantly.

## What Can Customers Do with RwG?

<CardGroup cols="2">
  <Card title="Book Directly from Search" icon="magnifying-glass">
    Customers can book a class or session without visiting your website — saving time and increasing conversion.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Real-Time Availability" icon="calendar">
    Your schedule is synced live with Google. Class times, instructors, and open spots are always up to date.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Mobile First Experience" icon="mobile">
    Bookings can be made directly from Google Maps and Assistant on Android and iOS.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Prepay Securely" icon="credit-card">

  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How to Get Started

Kenko handles the integration setup for you. If you're not already listed:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify Your Google Business Profile">
    Ensure your business is verified and visible on Google Maps and Search.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable RwG from Kenko">
    Go to `Website Management > Reserve With Google` and toggle it on.

    <Note>
      Kenko automatically turns on the Reserve with Google option for all verified businesses.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Your Classes and Pricing">
    Make sure your offerings are clear, correctly priced, and scheduled.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Kenko syncs your schedule with Google every few minutes — keeping availability always fresh.
</Info>
