# Marketing Campaigns

Kenko's campaign tools help you create targeted promotions to attract new members, engage existing ones, and drive business growth. This guide explains how to set up effective marketing campaigns within the platform.

## Creating a New Campaign

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Campaign Manager">
    Navigate to **Marketing** > **Campaigns** > **Campaign Manager**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start New Campaign">
    Click the **+ New Campaign** button.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Campaign Type">
    Choose from the available campaign types or select "Custom Campaign" to build from scratch.
    
    <Note>
      Pre-configured campaign types include recommended settings and templates to help you get started quickly.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Define Campaign Details">
    Enter the essential information:
    - Campaign name (internal reference)
    - Description and goals
    - Start and end dates
    - Budget (optional)
    - Team members responsible
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Target Audience">
    Define who will receive your campaign:
    - All members
    - Specific segments (based on membership type, activity level, etc.)
    - Custom audience (using filters and criteria)
    - Imported list (CSV upload)
    
    <Accordion icon="filter" title="Advanced Targeting Options">
      Create sophisticated audience segments using multiple criteria:
      
      - **Membership Criteria**: Type, status, expiration date
      - **Activity Metrics**: Visit frequency, class preferences, booking patterns
      - **Demographic Filters**: Age, location, interests
      - **Financial Factors**: Spending level, payment history
      - **Engagement Data**: Email open rates, app usage, previous campaign interactions
      
      Combine criteria using AND/OR logic for precise targeting.
    </Accordion>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Offer">
    Define what you're promoting:
    - Offer type (discount, free trial, etc.)
    - Offer details and value
    - Redemption methods
    - Usage limits and restrictions
    - Promotional codes (if applicable)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create Communication Plan">
    Set up how your campaign will be delivered:
    - Communication channels (email, SMS, in-app, etc.)
    - Message schedule and frequency
    - Content and creative assets
    - A/B test variations (optional)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Success Metrics">
    Define how you'll measure campaign performance:
    - Primary goal (new sign-ups, upgrades, etc.)
    - Key performance indicators (KPIs)
    - Target conversion rate
    - ROI expectations
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review and Launch">
    Preview all campaign elements, send test messages, and activate your campaign.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Campaign Communication Channels

<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion icon="desktop" title="Website Promotions">

    - Pop-up offers
    - Booking page upsells

## Tracking Campaign Performance

Monitor and analyze your campaign results:

1. Navigate to **Marketing** > **Campaigns** > **Analytics**
2. Select your campaign from the list
3. View performance metrics:
   - Delivery statistics (sends, opens, clicks)
   - Conversion tracking
   - Revenue attribution
   - ROI calculation

## Campaign Templates

Save time by using or adapting our pre-built campaign templates:

1. Navigate to **Marketing** > **Campaigns** > **Templates**
2. Browse templates by category or goal
3. Preview the template details
4. Click **Use Template** to create a new campaign based on the template
5. Customize as needed for your specific offer and audience
