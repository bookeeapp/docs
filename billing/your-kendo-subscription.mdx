# Managing Your Kenko Subscription

Kenko offers flexible subscription options to ensure you have the tools your business needs to succeed. Whether you're just starting out or scaling up, managing your Kenko subscription is simple and transparent.

## Available Plans and Offerings

Kenko offers multiple subscription plans tailored to the size and needs of your business. Each plan includes varying levels of features and access:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Starter Plan" icon="rocket">
    Ideal for new businesses or studios with minimal overhead.

    Features:

    - Core CRM and booking tools
    - Basic marketing automations
    - 1 Location
    - Up to 3 Staff Members

    Pricing available via the [Website Builder: Plans & Pricing](https://yourwebsite.com/plans-pricing).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Growth Plan" icon="trending-up">
    Suitable for expanding businesses.

    Features:

    - All Starter features
    - Advanced CRM & Retention tools
    - Unlimited staff members
    - Customizable website builder access
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Enterprise Plan" icon="building">
    Designed for large or multi-location enterprises.

    Features:

    - All Growth features
    - Multi-location management
    - Advanced reporting & analytics
    - Premium support SLAs
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Info>
  Full feature comparisons are listed in the [Kenko Website Builder: Plans, Pricing, and Features](https://yourwebsite.com/plans-pricing) documentation.

## Billing Settings

Kenko uses Stripe Connect for secure and flexible subscription billing.

### Recommended Billing Settings

- Ensure your Stripe Connect account is linked.
- Use automatic card payments for uninterrupted access.
- Enable payment notifications from Stripe.

<Note>
  Your subscription invoices will always be accessible from your Kenko Admin portal.
</Note>

## How to Upgrade or Downgrade Your Subscription

You can modify your subscription at any time directly through the Admin Portal.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Subscription Settings">
    Go to `Admin Settings > Billing > Manage Subscription`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select New Plan">
    Choose whether you want to upgrade for additional features or downgrade to a smaller plan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Changes">
    Changes will be prorated automatically by Stripe. Any difference will be billed or credited.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Some upgrades (like Enterprise features) may require additional setup or onboarding assistance.
</Warning>

## Pausing Your Kenko Subscription

If you need to temporarily pause your subscription:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Request Pause">
    Email `help@kenko.community` with your request.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Pause Terms">
    A team member will provide options such as pause duration and minimal holding fee (if any).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reactivate Later">
    Simply email again to reactivate your account when ready.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Pausing is recommended if you anticipate returning within a few months.
</Info>

## Cancelling Your Kenko Subscription

We hate to see you go\! If you must cancel:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Send Cancellation Request">
    Email `help@kenko.community` with the subject line: `Cancel Subscription`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Terms">
    Your subscription will be canceled at the end of your current billing period unless otherwise requested.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Data Retention">
    Your data may be retained securely for a limited time if you wish to rejoin later.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Canceling means you will immediately lose access to all services once the billing period ends.
</Warning>
