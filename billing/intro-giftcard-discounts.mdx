# Introduction to Gift Cards and Discounts

Kenko's platform offers robust tools for managing **gift cards** and **discounts**, enabling businesses to attract new customers, retain existing ones, and increase revenue. This guide provides an overview of their functionalities, use cases, and application areas.

## Gift Cards

Gift cards in Kenko serve as flexible payment options that can be used across various services and products. They are ideal for:

- **Gifting**: Customers can purchase gift cards for friends or family.
- **Promotions**: Businesses can issue gift cards as part of promotional campaigns.
- **Customer Retention**: Offering gift cards can encourage repeat visits.

## Discounts

Discounts in Kenko help in attracting new customers and incentivizing existing ones. They can be applied in various forms:

- **Percentage-Based**: e.g., 10% off on memberships.
- **Fixed Amount**: e.g., \$20 off on a specific service.
- **Promotional Codes**: Unique codes that customers can apply during checkout.
