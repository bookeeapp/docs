# Payroll Setup & Management

Kenko provides a robust Payroll system that allows you to automate compensation, manage pay periods, and ensure your staff is paid accurately and on time. This guide covers running payroll, templates, settings, and payroll history.

## Payroll Overview

The Payroll module is divided into four main areas

- **Run Payroll**
- **Payroll History**
- **Payroll Templates**
- **Payroll Settings**

Each section is designed to make your payroll management easy and compliant.

## ![Screenshot2025 04 28at11 58 03AM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-04-28at11.58.03AM.png)

Run Payroll

Running payroll is the final step where all staff compensations are calculated and processed.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Pay Period">
    Navigate to `Payroll > Run Payroll`.\
    Choose the pay period you want to finalize payroll for.

    ![Screenshot2025 04 28at12 00 52PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-04-28at12.00.52PM.png)

    ![Screenshot2025 04 28at11 59 19AM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-04-28at11.59.19AM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Draft Payroll">
    Check each staff’s

    - Total hours/classes
    - Bonuses, adjustments, and deductions
    - Deductions, Gross Pay and Reimbursements and Fixed Pay

    ![Screenshot2025 04 28at12 12 45PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-04-28at12.12.45PM.png)

    You can also edit it for an individual staff as follows. ( Editable items are Bonus, Tips, Other earnings, Deductions and Reimbusement )

    ![Screenshot2025 04 28at12 14 11PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-04-28at12.14.11PM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Payroll">
    Once finalized, Run the payroll. Once you make the payment to yout Staff and Instructors, you can record the payment date and Export the details for your records.

    <Note>
      Once submitted, it moves from **Draft** ➔ **Finalized** status.
    </Note>
    <Warning>
      Kenko does not record the payment and bank details of Staffs. You have to use a trhird party software like Quickbooks or Gusto to process the payment to your staff.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Payroll cannot be submitted until the pay period has ended to ensure complete and accurate data capture.
</Warning>

## Payroll Templates

Templates standardize how compensation is calculated.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Templates">
    Go to `Payroll > Payroll Templates` and click `Create Template`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Template Type">
    Choose based on staff role:

    - **Hourly Pay Rate**
    - **Per Class**
    - **Fixed Salary**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Pay Rates">
    Define base rates, overtime multipliers, and bonus eligibility.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply Templates">
    Assign templates to individual staff profiles or roles.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Hourly pay rate templates automatically calculate based on logged hours during the selected pay period.
</Info>

## Payroll Settings

Configure global settings that govern how payroll is calculated and run.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Pay Period Setup" icon="calendar-days">
    Define if pay runs weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly across locations.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Banking Information" icon="money-check-pen">

## Setting Staff Compensation

Compensation settings control individual payout configurations.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Staff Profile">
    Navigate to `Contacts > Staff > Compensation Settings`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Attach Payroll Template">
    Link the appropriate payroll template (Hourly, Per Class, Fixed).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Custom Overrides (Optional)">
    You can override default template values for specific staff members.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Staff compensation changes will take effect starting the next open pay period.
</Note>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why can't I run my payroll before the pay period ends?" icon="question-mark-circle">
    To ensure complete data integrity (attendance, hours, sales), payrolls are locked until the pay cycle is complete.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How does Hourly Pay calculate in payroll?" icon="calculator">
    Kenko automatically multiplies the hourly rate by the total logged hours in the period and applies overtime if configured.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I edit a payroll after submission?" icon="pencil">
    Only drafts can be edited. Once submitted, you must void and recreate if corrections are needed.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
