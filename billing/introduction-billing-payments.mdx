# Billings and Payments in Kenko

Kenko offers a robust billing and payment system designed to simplify financial management for fitness studios. This system encompasses various features, including flexible pricing options, introductory packs, tax management, invoicing, and seamless CRM integration.

## Pricing Options and Introductory Packs

Kenko provides flexible pricing models to accommodate diverse business needs:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Memberships" icon="credit-card">
    Offer recurring billing for regular clients, ensuring consistent revenue and client retention.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Introductory Packs" icon="gift">
    Attract new clients with discounted packages designed for first-time users. These packs can be limited in number or time, creating an urgency to purchase.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Credit Packs" icon="coins">
    Clients can purchase bundles of credits that can be used for classes or appointments, offering flexibility and prepayment options.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Top-ups" icon="arrow-up">
    Allow clients to add funds to their account for future use. These funds can be used for any service, making it easier for clients to manage their balance.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<AccordionGroup>
