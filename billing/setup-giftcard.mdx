# Everything You Need to Know About Gift Cards

Kenko's gift card system allows you to create and manage powerful gifting and promotional tools. Whether it's selling a card at the front desk, redeeming it online, or tracking balances, this guide will walk you through every workflow step by step.

## Setting Up Gift Cards in Bookee

To begin using gift cards, you'll first need to set them up under the Services section.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Create a Gift Card" icon="plus">
    1. Navigate to `Setup > Services > Gift card`
    2. Click **Create gift card**
    3. Fill in the following details:

    - Title (e.g. "\$250 Gift Card")
    - Value
    - Visibility (enable to show on app and website)
    - Redemptions (single-use or multi-use)

    1. Click **Create Gift Card** to save

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-04-25at4.04.10PM.png"
      alt="Create Gift Card"
      className="rounded-lg"
    />
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Single-Use vs Multi-Use" icon="tally-4">
    - **Single-Use**: One-time redemption. Balance is not carried forward.
    - **Multi-Use**: Balance remains available until fully used. Ideal for flexible, ongoing purchases.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Gift Card List" icon="gift">
    Once created, the Gift Card will appear in the list.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-04-25at4.05.50PM.png"
      alt="Gift Card List"
      className="rounded-lg"
    />
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How to Sell Gift Cards from POS

Gift cards can be issued from the Point of Sale during in-person interactions.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a Customer" icon="user-plus">
    From the POS screen, go to the **Gift Cards** tab. Choose a customer who is purchasing the gift card.\
    If they're not an existing member, create a new profile from the Contacts tab and return to the POS screen.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-04-25at4.08.36PM.png"
      alt="POS Gift Card Customer"
      className="rounded-lg"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a Gift Card">
    Select a gift card the customer wants to purchase.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-04-25at4.09.37PM.png"
      alt="Select Gift Card"
      className="rounded-lg"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Gift Card Type">
    Kenko supports both digital and physical gift card types. Each type has a slightly different configuration and use case during checkout.

    | Feature             | Digital Gift Card                                       | Physical Gift Card                                       |
    | ------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------- |
    | **Delivery Method** | Sent via email to the recipient                         | Handed out manually (requires physical inventory)        |
    | **Required Fields** | Recipient email, delivery date, optional message        | Gift card number, optional email                         |
    | **Scheduling**      | Can schedule for future delivery via date & time picker | Delivered instantly in person                            |
    | **Activation**      | Automatically activated on scheduled delivery           | Activated upon sale when gift card number is entered     |
    | **Use Case**        | Online gifting, marketing promotions                    | Walk-in customers, studio sales, branded gift card racks |

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-04-25at4.12.23PM.png"
      alt="Gift Card Physical Toggle"
      className="rounded-lg"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Receiver Details">
    - Fill in the **recipient's name** and **email**
    - Optionally add a **message**
    - Choose a **delivery date** or send immediately

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-04-25at4.12.14PM.png"
      alt="Digital Gift Card Entry"
      className="rounded-lg"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Sale">
    After adding to the cart, click **Charge** → choose payment method → click **Complete Payment**.\
    The gift card will be emailed to the recipient based on the selected schedule.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-04-25at4.18.14PM.png"
      alt="Gift Card Checkout"
      className="rounded-lg"
    />
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How Customers Purchase Gift Cards Online

Customers can purchase gift cards themselves from your website or app.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="From the Website" icon="globe">
    1. Visit the gift card store page
    2. Choose the gift card with the desired amount
    3. Enter recipient details, message, and delivery time
    4. Enter card details and complete the purchase
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="From the Customer App" icon="mobile">
    1. Go to **Store \> Gift Cards**
    2. Select a card amount
    3. Fill in recipient and payment details
    4. Tap **Buy Now** to send
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How Customers Redeem Gift Cards

Gift cards can be used both online and in person. Any unused balance is saved if the card is multi-use.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Online (Website Checkout)" icon="shopping-cart">
    1. Add any purchase items to the cart and click **Buy Now**
    2. On the payment screen, select **Redeem Gift Card**
    3. Enter the gift card code → click **Apply** → click **Confirm Purchase**

    If the gift card doesn't fully cover the purchase, the remaining balance will be charged to the saved/default payment method.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="In the Customer App" icon="mobile">
    1. Add the desired item and proceed to checkout
    2. On the payment page, select **Redeem Gift Card**
    3. Enter the code and complete the transaction
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="In Person (POS)" icon="store">
    1. Add items to the cart
    2. Select **Redeem Gift Card** as the payment method
    3. Enter the code → complete payment

    Partial redemptions are supported. Remaining balance will remain available if the card is multi-use.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How to View Gift Card Balance

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="For Admins" icon="eye">
    - Navigate to **Customer Profile \> Payments \> Saved Payment Methods**
    - View balance, redemption history, and refund logs
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="For Customers" icon="user">
    - Log in to website or app
    - Go to **Profile \> Saved Cards & Gift Cards**
    - See current balance and history
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Refunding a Gift Card Payment

Refunds for gift card purchases go back onto the gift card balance.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Payments Tab">
    In the customer profile, click on the **Payments** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Order">
    Click the relevant order and select **Refund Items**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Process Refund">
    Select payment mode as **Gift Card** → enter amount → click **Refund**.
    Confirm the refund. The amount will be added back to the original card balance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  If the original purchase was made using both gift card and card, only the gift card portion can be refunded back to the gift card.
</Warning>
