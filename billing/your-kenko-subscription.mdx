## Billing Settings

Kenko uses Stripe Connect for secure and flexible subscription billing.

### Recommended Billing Settings

- Ensure your Stripe Connect account is linked.
- Use automatic card payments for uninterrupted access.
- Enable payment notifications from Stripe.

<Note>
  Your subscription invoices are always accessible from your Kenko Admin portal.
</Note>

## How to Upgrade or Downgrade Your Subscription

You can modify your subscription at any time directly through the Admin Portal.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Subscription Settings">
    Go to `Admin Settings > Billing > Manage Subscription`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select New Plan">
    Choose whether you want to upgrade for additional features or downgrade to a smaller plan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Changes">
    Changes will be prorated automatically by Stripe. Any difference will be billed or credited.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Some upgrades (like Enterprise features) may require onboarding support or implementation assistance.
</Warning>

## Pausing Your Kenko Subscription

If you need to temporarily pause your subscription:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Request Pause">
    Email `help@kenko.community` with your request.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Pause Terms">
    A team member will provide pause duration options and any minimal holding fee.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reactivate Later">
    Simply email again to resume services when you're ready.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Cancelling Your Kenko Subscription

We hate to see you go\! If you must cancel:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Send Cancellation Request">
    Email `help@kenko.community` with the subject line: `Cancel Subscription`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Terms">
    Your subscription will be canceled at the end of your current billing period unless otherwise arranged.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Data Retention">
    Your data may be stored securely for a limited time if you decide to return.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Canceling means you will lose access to all features and data once the billing period ends.
</Warning>
