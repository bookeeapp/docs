# Introduction Billing Payments

Kenko offers a robust billing and payment system designed to simplify financial management for fitness studios. This system encompasses various features, including flexible pricing options, introductory packs, tax management, invoicing, and seamless CRM integration.

## Pricing Options and Introductory Packs

Kenko provides flexible pricing models to accommodate diverse business needs:

Offer recurring billing for regular clients, ensuring consistent revenue and client retention. Attract new clients with discounted packages designed for first-time users. These packs can be limited in number or time, creating an urgency to purchase. Clients can purchase bundles of credits that can be used for classes or appointments, offering flexibility and prepayment options. Allow clients to add funds to their account for future use. These funds can be used for any service, making it easier for clients to manage their balance.

## Tax Management

Kenko supports tax configurations to ensure compliance with local regulations. You can set up tax rates based on location and service type, automating tax calculations during checkout.

Apply different tax rates based on service or location to comply with local regulations. Taxes are automatically calculated during the checkout process to ensure accuracy. Create custom tax rules for specific services or regions to meet local legal requirements.

## Invoicing

Kenko's invoicing system enables the creation and management of invoices for clients. Features include:

Invoices are automatically generated when payment is received, saving time and reducing errors. Tailor invoice formats to match your studio's branding and client needs. Track paid and outstanding invoices, keeping your accounting up-to-date. Send digital receipts to clients for each transaction, ensuring clarity and transparency.

## CRM Billing Integration

Kenko’s integrated CRM and billing system allows for a seamless connection between your client management and billing processes. Key features include:

Maintain detailed records of client interactions, memberships, credits, and payment history. Set up recurring billing cycles for memberships and credit packs, ensuring smooth financial operations. Send payment reminders, invoices, and receipts directly to clients, keeping them informed. Clients can top-up their balance or purchase credit packs directly through the CRM interface, which is tracked for each client’s usage.

## Best Practices for Billings and Payments

Regularly evaluate your pricing models to ensure they align with customer needs and business goals. Offer clear and simple payment options to avoid confusion and improve the customer experience. Attract new clients with discounted introductory packs. This is a great way to build initial trust and encourage long-term memberships. Automate invoicing and payment reminders to reduce administrative tasks and ensure timely payments. Make sure your tax rates are up to date and configured correctly to comply with local regulations. Keep track of refunds issued to clients to maintain accurate financial records and customer satisfaction.
