# POS Deep Dive

Kenko's POS system is designed to help front-desk staff process sales quickly and confidently. From memberships to merchandise, the POS interface offers a unified checkout experience that's simple and powerful.

## What Can Be Sold on the POS?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Memberships" icon="id-card">

    _Trial: One-time use plans for first-time customers_

    _Shared: Can be assigned to multiple users (e.g. families)_

    _Multi-location: Valid at more than one studio or branch_

## Best Practices

<CardGroup cols="2">
  <Card title="Connect the Card Reader First" icon="plug">
    Always ensure your Stripe card reader is connected before initiating a sale. It helps avoid awkward delays during checkout.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Check Trial Eligibility" icon="exclamation-triangle">
    When adding trial memberships, POS will alert if the customer has used it before. Always verify before overriding.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Label Custom Charges Clearly" icon="tag">
    Whether it's a no-show fee or donation, always provide a clear label and optional note to maintain billing transparency.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Verify Identity Before Credit Use" icon="user-check">
    Before applying stored account credit or gift card balance, confirm the customer to ensure accurate redemption.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Tips for Staff

<Tip>
  Use customer notes to track past purchases and avoid offering repeat trials.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Shared memberships can be managed from the customer profile after checkout if contacts aren't available during the sale.
</Note>

<Warning>
  Make sure the Stripe card reader is connected before processing card transactions in-person.
</Warning>
