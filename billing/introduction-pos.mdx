# Kenko's Point of Sale (POS)

Kenko’s Point of Sale (POS) system empowers your front desk and staff to manage in-person sales with ease. Whether selling memberships, merchandise, or drop-in passes, Kenko provides a smooth checkout experience integrated into both the **sidebar** and **calendar** views.

## POS Access Points

<CardGroup cols="2">
  <Card title="POS from Sidebar" icon="store">
    Launch the full-featured POS from the sidebar for daily retail, membership, or service transactions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="POS in Calendar" icon="calendar">
    Trigger the POS directly from a class or appointment for quick session-based payments.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Sidebar POS Overview

The Sidebar POS is optimized for front-desk workflows and supports the full range of payment scenarios.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Launching Sidebar POS" icon="arrow-pointer">
    1. Login to the Kenko Admin Dashboard
    2. Click on **Point of Sale** in the sidebar navigation
    3. The POS module will open in full screen
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Supported Transactions" icon="bag-shopping">
    - Memberships & Class Packs
    - Drop-in Classes or Appointments
    - Merchandise and Retail Products
    - Custom Items or Fees (e.g., late cancel)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Features in Sidebar POS" icon="star">
    - Customer Lookup or Guest Checkout
    - Real-time Product Catalog
    - Apply Discounts (flat or %)
    - Accept Split Payments (e.g., Card \+ Cash)
    - Issue Digital or Printed Receipts
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## POS in Calendar

Use Calendar POS to charge attendees quickly, directly from class or session views.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How to Use Calendar POS" icon="calendar-check">
    1. Go to the **Calendar** tab
    2. Select an event or class
    3. Select one attendee’s name while booking them into the session
    4. The POS will facilitate the payment from the calendar
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Common Use Cases" icon="clock">
    - Charging drop-ins at check-in
    - Selling products after a session
    - Handling last-minute purchases
    - Selling Items/Services to Walk-in Customers
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Benefits of Calendar POS" icon="bolt">
    - Context-aware checkout
    - Direct link between attendance and billing
    - Faster front-desk flow during class transitions
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## POS Functional Breakdown

<CardGroup cols="2">
  <Card title="Add Items" icon="plus">
    Select memberships, class packs, merchandise, or custom charges. Each item appears in the cart for review.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Apply Discounts" icon="percent">
    Apply manual discounts or promo codes. Supports flat or percentage-based values.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Select Payment Method" icon="credit-card">
    Accept card, cash, Apple Pay, Google Pay, or use account credit. Supports split payments.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Complete & Track Sales" icon="receipt">
    Finalize the order, add notes, send/print receipt, and track in billing history or reporting modules.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Verify Customer Identity" icon="user-check">
    Always confirm the correct customer profile before using account credit or charging a membership.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Use Contextual POS" icon="rotate">
    - Prefer Calendar POS for session-related charges
    - Use Sidebar POS for general purchases or walk-ins.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Encourage Digital Receipts" icon="envelope">
    Offer digital receipts by default to streamline experience and reduce paper use.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Audit Sales Regularly" icon="clipboard-check">
    Use **Billing Reports** to reconcile cash transactions and detect discrepancies.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
