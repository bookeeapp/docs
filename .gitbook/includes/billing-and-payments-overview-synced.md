## Pricing options

Pick the pricing model that matches how you sell access.

* **Recurring memberships**: predictable monthly revenue.
* **Intro packs**: discounted first-time offers.
* **Credit packs**: prepaid credits for classes or appointments.
* **Top-ups**: store value on an account for future purchases.

## Tax management

Set tax rules once, then let checkout handle the math.

* Apply different rates by **location** or **service type**.
* Calculate taxes automatically at checkout.
* Add custom rules for special services or regions.

## Invoicing & receipts

Invoices and receipts keep payments auditable and easy to track.

* Auto-generate invoices when payments succeed.
* Customize invoice details to match your brand.
* Track paid vs outstanding invoices.
* Send digital receipts per transaction.

## CRM + billing integration

Billing is tied directly to contact profiles, so staff sees the full context.

* View memberships, credits, and payment history in one place.
* Run recurring billing for memberships and packs.
* Send reminders, invoices, and receipts automatically.
* Let clients buy packs or top-up, with usage tracked per contact.

## Best practices

* Review pricing quarterly. Adjust based on retention and utilization.
* Keep checkout options simple. Reduce friction.
* Use intro packs to convert leads faster.
* Automate invoices and reminders. Avoid manual chasing.
* Audit tax rules when you add locations or new services.
* Track refunds consistently. Keep reporting accurate.
