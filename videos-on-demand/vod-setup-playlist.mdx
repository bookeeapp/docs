# What are Playlists ?

Kenko's **Playlists** feature lets you curate a collection of on-demand videos into structured learning paths, fitness programs, or themed bundles. Whether you’re building a multi-day challenge, a recovery flow series, or simply organizing by category, playlists give your content greater visibility and accessibility for your members.

## Best Practices for Playlists

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use Categories Wisely" icon="folder-tree">
    Categorize playlists (e.g. strength, mobility, prenatal) to help users find relevant content faster.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Add Clear Descriptions" icon="text">
    Use the description field to outline what the playlist covers, duration, difficulty level, and what users can expect.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Prioritize Thumbnail Quality" icon="image">
    Use high-resolution thumbnails that reflect the playlist theme — consistency builds brand trust.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Update Regularly" icon="rotate">
    Add new videos to evergreen playlists and promote seasonal or challenge-based series on a rolling basis.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

By using playlists effectively, you increase the discoverability, structure, and monetization potential of your on-demand library. It's one of the most powerful ways to keep your clients engaged and coming back for more.
