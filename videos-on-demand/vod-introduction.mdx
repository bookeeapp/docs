# Introduction to Videos on Demand (VOD)

Kenko's **Videos on Demand (VOD)** offering allows fitness, wellness, and education businesses to expand their reach beyond physical locations by offering pre-recorded content accessible anytime, anywhere.

Whether you are a yoga studio, personal trainer, or wellness coach, VOD empowers you to deliver value even when your clients can't attend live sessions.

## What Kenko's VOD System Offers

Kenko provides a full-featured, flexible VOD platform integrated right into your client portal and app experience.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Seamless Integration" icon="link">
    Upload videos directly from your Admin Panel and organize them under categories such as "Yoga Basics," "Pilates Advanced," "Meditation Sessions," etc.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Access Control" icon="lock">
    Control who can view your VOD library

    - Paid members only
    - Free preview content
    - Pay-per-video options
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Monetization Options" icon="dollar-sign">
    - Include VOD access as part of memberships
    - Sell one-off access to specific videos
    - Bundle with course sales
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Device Compatibility" icon="mobile">
    Your clients can watch VOD on desktop, mobile, or tablets seamlessly.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Analytics" icon="chart-bar">
    Track views, popular videos, average watch time, and client engagement metrics.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How to Get Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable VOD Module">
    Request VOD activation through your Kenko Customer Success Manager or email `help@kenko.community`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload Your Content">
    Go to `Admin > Videos > Upload` to start organizing your content library.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Access Rules">
    Decide if videos are

    - Free
    - Members-only
    - Pay-per-view
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish and Promote">
    Feature your VOD section in newsletters, app banners, and on your studio website.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Kenko's VOD library supports high-definition video uploads with adaptive streaming to ensure the best client experience.
</Info>
