# VOD Deep Dive

Kenko's **Video On Demand (VOD)** feature allows you to create a flexible digital content library for your customers. Whether it's yoga sessions, educational content, fitness tutorials, or exclusive workshops â€” VOD lets you deliver videos that members can access anytime, from anywhere.

This guide walks you through everything you need: setup, uploading videos, editing, playlists, and pricing options.

## What is VOD in Kenko?

Kenko's VOD system allows you to

- Upload pre-recorded videos
- Organize videos into playlists
- Control video access (free, paid, rental, membership-based)
- Sell or rent content to customers or members
- Provide on-demand learning or fitness experiences

Kenko supports both **individual videos** and **curated playlists**, enabling studios and businesses to monetize their content library easily.

## When Should You Use VOD?

_Use VOD if you want to_

- Offer flexible workout or learning options
- Provide exclusive content to paying members
- Rent or sell classes without needing live sessions
- Add value to your membership plans
- Extend your offerings internationally without time zone constraints

## Editing Existing Videos

To edit a video:

1. Click `Edit` next to the video.
2. Modify title, description, instructors, pricing, thumbnail, or publish status.
3. Click `Save Changes`.

# Pricing and Access Options

| Option     | Description                                                          |
| ---------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Free       | Accessible to everyone without payment.                              |
| Paid       | Restricted to customers with specific memberships.                   |
| Rental     | Pay-per-video access for a specific duration (days/weeks/months).    |
| Membership | Only viewable for active members of the selected subscription plans. |

<Info>
  Paid and rental videos unlock additional revenue streams from your content library.
</Info>
