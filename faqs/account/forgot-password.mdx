# How to Reset Your Forgotten Password

If you've forgotten your password for the Kenko platform, you can easily reset it by following these steps.

## For Business Owners and Staff

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Login Page">
    Visit [app.gokenko.com](https://app.gokenko.com) or open your Kenko mobile app.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click 'Forgot Password'">
    On the login screen, click the **Forgot Password?** link below the login button.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Your Email">
    Enter the email address associated with your Kenko account and click **Send Reset Link**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Check Your Email">
    Open your email inbox and look for a message from Kenko with the subject "Reset Your Kenko Password." 
    
    <Note>
      If you don't see the email within a few minutes, check your spam or junk folder.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click the Reset Link">
    In the email, click the **Reset Password** button or link. This will open a browser window with a password reset form.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create New Password">
    Enter your new password twice to confirm it. Your password must be at least 8 characters long and include a combination of letters, numbers, and special characters.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Login with New Password">
    After successfully resetting your password, you'll be redirected to the login page. Enter your email and new password to access your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## For Members Using the Client Portal

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Your Studio's Booking Page">
    Visit your studio's booking page or open the studio's mobile app.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click 'Login'">
    Click the **Login** button in the top right corner of the screen.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select 'Forgot Password'">
    On the login popup, click the **Forgot Password?** link.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Follow Password Reset Steps">
    Enter your email address and follow the same reset process described above.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting Password Reset Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="envelope" title="Didn't Receive Reset Email">
    If you haven't received the password reset email after 15 minutes:
    
    1. Check your spam/junk folder
    2. Verify you entered the correct email address
    3. Add support@gokenko.com to your contacts
    4. Try requesting another reset link
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="clock" title="Reset Link Expired">
    Password reset links expire after 24 hours for security reasons. If your link has expired, simply return to the login page and request a new reset link.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="exclamation-triangle" title="Email Not Recognized">
    If the system doesn't recognize your email address, it could mean:
    
    - You might be using a different email than the one registered
    - Your account may have been created with an alternative email
    - Your account might not exist yet
    
    Contact your studio administrator or Kenko support for assistance.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Password Security Tips

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Strong Password Creation" icon="lock">
    Create a unique password that includes uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Avoid Password Reuse" icon="fingerprint">
    Use a different password for Kenko than you use for other services.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Consider a Password Manager" icon="key">
    Use a password manager to securely store and generate strong passwords.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Regular Updates" icon="sync">
    Change your password periodically, especially if you suspect any security issues.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
