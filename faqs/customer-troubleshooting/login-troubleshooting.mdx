## Step-by-step guide to recover a Customer's Account

When customers face difficulties logging into their accounts, it’s important to provide clear, efficient solutions to ensure a smooth user experience. Follow the steps below to troubleshoot and resolve common login problems.

### Step 1: Check if the Customer Account is Archived

**Problem**: The customer's account may be archived, preventing them from logging in.

**How to Troubleshoot**:

1. **Locate the Customer’s Account**:
   Use the search function within the CRM to find the specific customer account.

   ![Screenshot2025 04 08at1 27 39PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-04-08at1.27.39PM.png)
2. \*\*Check Account Status: \*\*Verify is the account is archived. Archived accounts are dispplayed in a separate tab in the customer list.
3. **Unarchive the Account**:
   If the account is archived, change its status to active by unarchiving it. This action will restore the customer's access.
   ![Screenshot2025 04 08at1 30 47PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-04-08at1.30.47PM.png)

### Step 2: Reset the Customer's Password via Bookee

**Problem**: The customer might have forgotten their password or is using the wrong password.

**How to Troubleshoot**:

1. **Reset Password**: In Bookee, use the option to reset the customer's password. You can either enter a temporary password manually.

   ![Screenshot2025 04 08at1 36 31PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-04-08at1.36.31PM.png)
2. **Communicate the New Password**:
   Save the changes and securely communicate the temporary password to the customer.

   **Important**: Advise the customer to change the temporary password immediately after logging in for security purposes.

### Step 3: Guide the Customer to Reset Their Password Using the "Forgot Password" Feature

**Problem**: The customer is still unable to log in despite the reset password or prefers to use a self-service option.

**How to Troubleshoot**:

1. **Direct to Forgot Password**:
   Instruct the customer to go to the login page and click on the "Forgot Password" link.

   ![Screenshot2025 04 08at1 37 59PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-04-08at1.37.59PM.png)

   ![Screenshot2025 04 08at1 38 07PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-04-08at1.38.07PM.png)
2. **Security Code Verification**: Explain that clicking on the "Forgot Password" link will trigger the system to send a security code to the customer’s email.
3. **Follow the Instructions**:
   Once the customer receives the security code, they should enter it in the designated field on the reset password page. After entering the code, they will be prompted to create a new password.

   **Tip**: Ensure they choose a strong password for added security.

### Additional Tips for Successful Troubleshooting

1. **Verify Contact Information**:
   Ensure that the customer’s phone number and email address are up-to-date in Bookee. If they are incorrect, update them before proceeding with the password reset.
2. **Communicate Clearly**:
   Provide clear, step-by-step instructions to the customer throughout the process. Patience and clear communication can significantly enhance the customer's experience.
3. **Follow Up**:
   After resolving the issue, follow up with the customer to ensure they are able to log in and access their account without any further problems.

By following these steps, you can effectively troubleshoot and resolve common customer login issues, ensuring a seamless and satisfactory experience for your users.
