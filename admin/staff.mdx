# Managing Staffs and Roles

Managing your staff efficiently is essential for smooth business operations. Kenko allows admins to add new staff, configure detailed profiles, and empower staff members to manage aspects of their availability and compensation via the Business App.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add New Staff">
    **Navigate to Staff â†’ Add Staff. **

    ![Screenshot2025 05 15at6 28 42PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-05-15at6.28.42PM.png)

    Enter the staff's basic details such as email, name, phone, gender, pronouns, date of birth, and address.

    ![Screenshot2025 05 15at6 29 25PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-05-15at6.29.25PM.png)

    _Upload a profile image to personalize the staff account._
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Permissions and Roles">
    Assign permissions like Instructor privileges or lead assignment.

    The roles chosen in "Roles and Privileges" can be assigned here, this will determne the access to the CRM for the Staff.

    ![Screenshot2025 05 15at6 29 40PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-05-15at6.29.40PM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Staff Profile">
    Click Add Staff to create the profile this triggers an email that is sent to the Staff for Password reset and verification. It is also possible to set a password from the CRM itself.

    ![Screenshot2025 05 15at6 29 56PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-05-15at6.29.56PM.png)

    You can additionally, Archive, Edit, Message a Staff from their profile here.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit Appointment Availability">
    Staff's Appointment availability can be configured from here, Kenko will automatically alert if any conflict comes with the Staff's other commitments
  </Step>
  <Step title="Setup Staff Compensation">
    You can configure the Staff's compensation structure based on the Service they provide from here.

    Learn more about setting of compensation here.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Staff Public Bio">
    Customers prefer Staffs with a Public Bio to get more information about the Staff. This Bio appears on the Customer App for the Customers to see.

    ![Screenshot2025 05 15at6 30 48PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-05-15at6.30.48PM.png)

    We recomend adding the Social Links of you Staff ( Especially if they are the instructors ) as well if they consent.

    ![Screenshot2025 05 15at6 30 58PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-05-15at6.30.58PM.png)

    _We support Instagram, Meta, Spotify and Tiktok Profiles._
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Best practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Grant only necessary permissions" icon="plus">
    Only assign permissions essential for each role to maintain security and reduce errors.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Keep availability up to date" icon="calendar">
    Encourage staff to regularly update their availability to prevent scheduling conflicts.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Use public bios and social links" icon="instagram">
    Help customers connect with staff by adding bios and social media profiles with consent.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Review roles regularly" icon="eye">
    Periodically audit staff roles and permissions to align with evolving business needs.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Maintain clear communication" icon="megaphone">
    Keep your team informed about role changes and schedule updates to avoid confusion.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
