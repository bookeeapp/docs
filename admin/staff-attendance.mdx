# How is Attendace Recorded?

Staff Attendance enables you to efficiently track employee clock-ins, absences, and attendance reports.

## Features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Clock-in/Clock-out" icon="clock">
    - Track clock-in/out via CRM or the Business App, Staffs can check-in when they come in.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Attendance Tracker" icon="eye">
    - You can track the total hours of the Staff based on their check-in and check-out times.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Attendance Reports" icon="file">
    - Attendance reports for payroll and compliance. 
  </Card>
  <Card title="Integrated with Payroll" icon="money-bill">
    - Integration with payroll and HR systems, based on overall hours at the studio.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How to Use

<Steps>
  <Step title="Recording Attendance">
    Employees clock in and out via the portal or business app. Managers can view their clock-in & clock-out time via the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitoring Attendance">
    Use the attendance dashboard to view real-time attendance. Staffs can be manually clocked-in via the CRM dashboard as well.

    ![image.png](/images/image.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reporting">
    Generate and export reports directly into excel sheet or CSV.

  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Clear Communication" icon="megaphone">
    Ensure all employees understand attendance policies clearly to avoid confusion. Make it clear that these clock-in and clock-out time might directly affect their compensation.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Regular Review" icon="eye">
    Periodically review attendance records to identify absenteeism or lateness early.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
