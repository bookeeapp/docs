# Introduction

Kenko provides a comprehensive suite of marketing tools designed specifically for fitness businesses. These tools help you attract new members, retain existing ones, and grow your revenue.

## Kenko Journeys

Automate your client engagement with our powerful workflow builder. Create personalized customer journeys that nurture leads and keep members engaged.

Automatically follow up with clients who purchase intro offers to convert them to full members. Re-engage clients who haven't visited your studio in a while with automated campaigns. Celebrate client milestones automatically to increase retention and loyalty. Create complex automation workflows with our intuitive drag-and-drop interface.

## Referral Program

Our cashless, mobile-first referral program makes it easy for your members to refer friends and family to your studio.

Members can refer friends directly from their phones with a seamless mobile experience. Automatically track and reward successful referrals without any manual intervention. Our referral program has driven over \*\*\\$100,000\*\* in new sales for our partner studios.

## Lead Capture & Sales Pipeline

Never let a potential member fall through the cracks with our comprehensive lead management system.

Monitor every lead from first contact to membership with detailed tracking. Visualize your sales funnel and easily identify bottlenecks or drop-off points. Set up automatic follow-up sequences for leads based on their pipeline stage.

## Newsletters & Outreach

Communicate effectively with your audience using our email marketing tools.

Choose from a variety of professionally designed email templates to match your branding. Create, schedule, and manage email campaigns for special promotions or announcements. Track open rates, click-through rates, and conversions to refine your outreach strategy.

## AI Inbox

Bring all your communication channels together in one place with our AI-powered inbox.

Manage emails, SMS, and app messages in a single platform. Get smart suggestions for quick responses and next best actions. Assign conversations, collaborate with teammates, and track resolutions efficiently.![AI Inbox](../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot2025-04-07at12.08.45PM.png)

## Community Posts

Engage your community with in-app posts that get 7x better response rates than traditional emails.

## Gift Cards & Promo Codes

Drive new business with customizable gift cards and promotional offers.

Allow members to purchase and send digital gift cards effortlessly. Create targeted promotional codes for special campaigns and track performance.
