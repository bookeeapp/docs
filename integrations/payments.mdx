Kenko integrates with leading payment processors to provide secure, reliable payment processing for your fitness business. These integrations allow you to accept various payment methods, manage recurring billing, and provide a seamless checkout experience for your clients.

Stripe is a comprehensive payment processing platform that enables businesses to accept payments online and in person.

### Features

- **Multiple Payment Methods**: Accept credit cards, debit cards, ACH transfers, and more
- **Recurring Billing**: Automatically charge clients for memberships and payment plans
- **Secure Checkout**: PCI-compliant payment processing for maximum security
- **International Payments**: Accept payments in multiple currencies
- **Detailed Reporting**: Access comprehensive payment analytics

### Setup Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Stripe Account">
    If you don't already have one, sign up for a Stripe account at [stripe.com](https://stripe.com)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect to Kenko">
    In your Kenko dashboard, go to Settings > Integrations > Payments and select Stripe
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize Connection">
    Follow the prompts to authorize Kenko to connect with your Stripe account
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Settings">
    Set your preferred payment methods, currencies, and other options
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best Practices for Payment Integrations

To get the most out of your payment integrations with Kenko, follow these best practices:

1. **Enable Multiple Payment Methods**: Offer various payment options to accommodate client preferences
2. **Set Up Automated Dunning**: Configure automatic retry attempts for failed payments
3. **Implement Secure Authentication**: Use 3D Secure for additional fraud protection
4. **Monitor Transaction Reports**: Regularly review payment analytics to identify trends
5. **Keep Your Integration Updated**: Ensure you're using the latest version of the integration

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues with your payment integrations, try these common solutions:

- **Connection Issues**: Verify your API credentials are correct and up to date
- **Failed Payments**: Check that the payment method information is valid and has sufficient funds
- **Webhook Errors**: Ensure webhook endpoints are properly configured
- **Currency Mismatches**: Confirm that you've enabled the correct currencies in both Kenko and your payment processor
