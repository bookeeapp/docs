# Connecting Your Stripe Account

Kenko partners with Stripe to provide secure, reliable payment processing for your fitness business. This guide will walk you through connecting your Stripe account to Kenko.

## Before You Begin

Before connecting Stripe to Kenko, make sure you have:

1. An active Kenko account with administrative privileges
2. A Stripe account (or be prepared to create one)
3. Business details including:
   - Legal business name
   - Business address
   - Tax ID number
   - Banking information for deposits

<Note>
  If you don't already have a Stripe account, you'll be able to create one during the connection process.
</Note>

## Connection Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Payment Settings">
    In your Kenko dashboard, navigate to **Admin Settings** > **Billing & Payments** > **Payment Processing**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Stripe">
    In the Payment Processors section, click **Connect** next to the Stripe option.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Connection Type">
    Select one of the following options:
    - **Connect existing Stripe account**: Link your current Stripe account
    - **Create new Stripe account**: Set up a new Stripe account through Kenko
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Authorize Connection">
    You'll be redirected to Stripe's website to:
    
    1. Login to your existing Stripe account (or create a new one)
    2. Review the permissions Kenko is requesting
    3. Authorize the connection
    
    <Warning>
      Ensure you're granting access to the correct Stripe account, especially if you manage multiple businesses.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Account Setup">
    If this is a new Stripe account, you'll need to provide additional information:
    
    - Business details and address
    - Banking information for deposits
    - Identity verification documents
    
    Existing accounts may already have this information, but verify it's correct.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Payment Settings">
    After connecting, return to Kenko to configure your payment settings:
    
    1. Default payment methods to accept
    2. Automatic billing retry settings
    3. Payment failure notifications
    4. Receipt and invoice customization
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Testing Your Connection

After connecting Stripe, we recommend testing the integration:

1. Go to **Admin Settings** > **Billing & Payments** > **Test Mode**
2. Toggle **Test Mode** to ON
3. Perform a test transaction using Stripe's [test card numbers](https://stripe.com/docs/testing):
   - Test successful payment: 4242 4242 4242 4242
   - Test failed payment: 4000 0000 0000 0002
4. Verify the test transaction appears in both Kenko and your Stripe dashboard
5. Toggle **Test Mode** OFF when finished testing

<Warning>
  Always remember to turn off Test Mode before processing real transactions.
</Warning>

## Common Connection Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="exclamation-circle" title="Connection Timeout">
    If your connection times out, try again and ensure you complete all steps on the Stripe site without delays.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="id-card" title="Verification Problems">
    If Stripe requires additional verification, complete all requested steps in your Stripe dashboard before attempting to reconnect.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="link-slash" title="Disconnection Issues">
    If your Stripe account becomes disconnected, check for:
    - Expired API keys
    - Account restrictions in Stripe
    - Changes to your Stripe account status
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="exchange-alt" title="Multiple Account Confusion">
    If you have multiple Stripe accounts, ensure you're connecting the correct one by checking the email address associated with the account during authorization.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Managing Your Stripe Connection

### Viewing Connected Account

To view your connected Stripe account:

1. Go to **Admin Settings** > **Billing & Payments** > **Payment Processing**
2. Look for the "Connected Account" section to see which Stripe account is connected

### Disconnecting Stripe

If you need to disconnect your Stripe account:

1. Go to **Admin Settings** > **Billing & Payments** > **Payment Processing**
2. Click **Disconnect** next to your Stripe connection
3. Confirm the disconnection

<Warning>
  Disconnecting your Stripe account will prevent all future payments from processing. Only disconnect if you're switching payment processors or closing your account.
</Warning>
