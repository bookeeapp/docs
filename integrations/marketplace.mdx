# Marketplace Integrations

Kenko integrates with leading fitness marketplaces to help you reach new clients and maximize your studio's capacity. These integrations allow you to list your classes and appointments on popular platforms, attracting new clients while maintaining full control over your schedule and pricing.

Gympass integration connects your Kenko schedule with the Gympass corporate wellness platform, giving you access to employees from thousands of companies worldwide.

### Features

- **Corporate Client Access**: Reach employees from Gympass partner companies
- **Automated Schedule Sync**: Your class schedule automatically appears on Gympass
- **Seamless Check-In**: Gympass users can check in to your classes with their app
- **Flexible Capacity Management**: Control how many spots you allocate to Gympass users
- **Simplified Billing**: Receive consolidated payments from Gympass

### Setup Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Apply to Gympass">
    Contact Gympass to apply as a partner through your Kenko dashboard
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Onboarding">
    Work with your Gympass representative to complete the onboarding process
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect to Kenko">
    In your Kenko dashboard, go to Settings > Integrations > Marketplace and select Gympass
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Settings">
    Set your capacity limits, class availability, and other preferences for Gympass users
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Setup Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Apply to ClassPass">
    Sign up as a ClassPass partner through your Kenko dashboard
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Partner Profile">
    Create your studio profile with descriptions, photos, and amenities
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect to Kenko">

## Benefits of Marketplace Integrations

Integrating with fitness marketplaces through Kenko offers numerous advantages:

- **Fill Empty Spots**: Generate revenue from classes that would otherwise have vacant spots
- **Reach New Clients**: Access users who might not find your studio through traditional marketing
- **Zero Marketing Costs**: Marketplaces handle the promotion of your classes to their users
- **Simplified Management**: Manage all marketplace integrations from your Kenko dashboard
- **Conversion Opportunity**: Convert marketplace users to direct members of your studio

## Best Practices for Marketplace Integrations

To get the most out of your marketplace integrations with Kenko, follow these best practices:

1. **Start with Limited Inventory**: Begin by offering a small number of spots to marketplace users
2. **Optimize Class Times**: Focus on offering off-peak times to marketplace users
3. **Create a Great First Experience**: Ensure marketplace users have an exceptional first visit
4. **Have a Conversion Strategy**: Develop a plan to convert marketplace users to direct members
5. **Monitor Performance**: Regularly review which classes perform best on marketplaces

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues with your marketplace integrations, try these common solutions:

- **Schedule Sync Issues**: Verify that your class schedule is correctly set up in Kenko
- **Capacity Problems**: Check that you haven't exceeded your allocated spots for marketplace users
- **User Check-In Difficulties**: Ensure your staff knows how to process check-ins from marketplace users
- **Payment Discrepancies**: Reconcile marketplace reports with your Kenko dashboard

For additional assistance, contact [Kenko Support](mailto:support@gokenko.com).
