## Benefits of Kenko Integrations

Integrating your fitness business with third-party services through Kenko offers numerous advantages:

- **Streamlined Operations**: Automate workflows and reduce manual tasks
- **Enhanced Client Experience**: Provide seamless experiences across platforms
- **Expanded Reach**: Access new client bases through marketplace integrations
- **Increased Revenue**: Unlock additional revenue streams
- **Data Insights**: Gather comprehensive data across all your business tools

## Getting Started with Integrations

Setting up integrations in Kenko is simple:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Integrations">
    Navigate to Settings > Integrations in your Kenko dashboard
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Integration">
    Choose the integration you want to set up from the available options
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Accounts">
    Follow the prompts to connect your third-party account with Kenko
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Settings">
    Customize the integration settings to match your business needs
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Available Integrations

Kenko currently supports the following integrations:

### Payments
- [Stripe](/integrations/payments#stripe) - Payment processing platform
- [Adyen Payments](/integrations/payments#adyen-payments) - Payment processing platform

### Scheduling
- [Google Calendar](/integrations/scheduling#google-calendar) - Sync events to instructor's personal calendar
- [Zoom](/integrations/scheduling#zoom) - Online classes and appointments

### Marketing
- [Google Analytics](/integrations/marketing#google-analytics) - Track website traffic and user behavior
- [Meta Pixel](/integrations/marketing#meta-pixel) - Ad tracking and retargeting tool
- [Mailchimp](/integrations/marketing#mailchimp) - Sync your contacts and send marketing emails
- [Zapier](/integrations/marketing#zapier) - Push data between apps automatically

### Marketplace
- [Gympass](/integrations/marketplace#gympass) - Unlock new revenue streams through corporate clients
- [ClassPass](/integrations/marketplace#classpass) - Sell your class & appointment spots on ClassPass
- [MyClubs](/integrations/marketplace#myclubs) - The leading sports aggregator in Austria and Switzerland
