# ClassPass Integration

ClassPass is a global fitness discovery platform that helps thousands of users find, book, and attend classes every day. When integrated with Kenko, it becomes an effortless channel to increase visibility, fill classes and appointments, and acquire new clients — all while keeping your operations lean.

## Why Use ClassPass?

- List your services in front of a massive, fitness-focused user base
- Backfill empty spots in your schedule with zero additional marketing
- Automate bookings, check-ins, and client records — less manual work
- Get paid per visit with consolidated, no-hassle monthly payouts

## Setting Up the ClassPass Integration

<Steps>
  <Step title="Apply to ClassPass" icon="user-plus">
    Visit the [ClassPass Partner Page](https://classpass.com/partners/studio-fitness) and submit an application to become a partner studio.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Your Studio Profile" icon="user">
    Set up your studio with branding, descriptions, photos, and service types. This is what potential clients will see on the ClassPass app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect to Kenko" icon="plug">
    In your Kenko dashboard, go to **Apps → Marketplace**, select **ClassPass**, and follow the integration prompts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Availability" icon="wrench">
    Decide which classes or appointments to publish, and how many spots to offer. You have full control over availability.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Accepting Bookings" icon="circle-check">
    Once connected, ClassPass users can browse, book, and check in — with everything logged in Kenko automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Integration Nuances

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Which service types are supported?" icon="list">
    Both group classes and appointments can be listed on ClassPass. Appointments must have **spot booking enabled with auto-assignment** to be eligible.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do ClassPass users show up in Kenko?" icon="users">
    Users are automatically added to your Kenko CRM when they book. They’ll be tagged as **ClassPass** clients, and their email address will indicate they came from ClassPass.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What does the booking and check-in flow look like?" icon="calendar-check">
    All ClassPass bookings sync into your Kenko schedule in real time. Clients must check in through the ClassPass app or be marked manually in Kenko to confirm attendance.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How is payment handled?" icon="credit-card">
    You get paid per verified visit. Payments are calculated and processed directly by ClassPass, and paid out monthly — no invoicing needed.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## When to Use This Integration

- You're looking to increase visibility without increasing ad spend
- You want to fill last-minute openings and under-booked slots
- You offer services that suit fitness trial seekers (intro packs, beginner classes, etc.)
- You’re opening a new location and want instant exposure
