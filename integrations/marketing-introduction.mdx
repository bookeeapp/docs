# Introduction to Marketing Integrations

Kenko integrates with leading marketing tools to help you promote your fitness business, attract new clients, and retain existing ones. These integrations enable you to track website performance, run targeted advertising campaigns, manage email marketing, and automate marketing workflows.

## Google Analytics

  className="block dark:hidden"

<img

  alt="Google Analytics"
  width="200"
/>

Google Analytics integration allows you to track website traffic, user behavior, and conversion metrics for your Kenko booking pages.

### Features

- **Comprehensive Tracking**: Monitor website traffic, page views, and user behavior
- **Conversion Tracking**: Track bookings, purchases, and other conversion events
- **Audience Insights**: Gain valuable insights about your website visitors
- **Campaign Measurement**: Evaluate the performance of your marketing campaigns
- **Custom Reports**: Create tailored reports for your specific business needs

### Setup Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Google Analytics Account">
    If you don't already have one, sign up for a Google Analytics account at [analytics.google.com](https://analytics.google.com)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect to Kenko">
    In your Kenko dashboard, go to Settings \> Integrations \> Marketing and select Google Analytics
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Tracking ID">
    Enter your Google Analytics tracking ID (UA-XXXXXXXX-X or G-XXXXXXXXXX)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Tracking Options">
    Select which events you want to track and customize your tracking settings
  </Step>
</Steps>

  alt="Meta Pixel"

Meta Pixel integration enables you to track website visitors, optimize ad delivery, and build targeted audiences for your Facebook and Instagram ads.

### Features

- **Conversion Tracking**: Track actions people take on your website
- **Audience Building**: Create custom audiences based on website visitors
- **Ad Optimization**: Improve ad targeting and delivery
- **Retargeting**: Show ads to people who have visited your website
- **Campaign Measurement**: Measure the effectiveness of your Facebook and Instagram ads

### Setup Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Meta Business Account">
    If you don't already have one, create a Meta Business account at [business.facebook.com](https://business.facebook.com)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a Pixel">
    In your Meta Business Manager, create a new pixel for your fitness business
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect to Kenko">
    In your Kenko dashboard, go to Settings \> Integrations \> Marketing and select Meta Pixel
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Pixel ID">
    Enter your Meta Pixel ID and configure the events you want to track
  </Step>
</Steps>

Mailchimp integration allows you to sync your Kenko contacts with your email marketing platform, create targeted campaigns, and automate client communications.

### Features

- **Contact Synchronization**: Automatically sync client information with Mailchimp
- **Segmentation**: Create targeted segments based on client activity
- **Automated Emails**: Set up automated emails for class bookings, cancellations, and more
- **Campaign Creation**: Design and send professional email campaigns
- **Performance Tracking**: Monitor open rates, click-through rates, and conversions

### Setup Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Mailchimp Account">
    If you don't already have one, sign up for a Mailchimp account at [mailchimp.com](https://mailchimp.com)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect to Kenko">
    In your Kenko dashboard, go to Settings \> Integrations \> Marketing and select Mailchimp
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize Connection">
    Follow the prompts to authorize Kenko to connect with your Mailchimp account
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Sync Settings">
    Select which client information to sync and how often to update your lists
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Steps>

  </Step>

    In your Kenko dashboard, go to Settings \> Integrations \> Marketing and select Zapier
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize Connection">
    Follow the prompts to authorize Kenko to connect with your Zapier account
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Zaps">
    Build automated workflows that connect Kenko with your other marketing tools
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues with your marketing integrations, try these common solutions:

- **Connection Issues**: Verify your API credentials are correct and up to date
- **Data Sync Problems**: Check that your field mappings are correctly configured
- **Automation Failures**: Ensure trigger events are properly set up
- **Performance Concerns**: Optimize your workflows to reduce unnecessary operations

For additional assistance, contact [Kenko Support](mailto:support@gokenko.com).
