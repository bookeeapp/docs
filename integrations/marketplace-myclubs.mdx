# MyClubs Integration

MyClubs is a leading fitness aggregator in Austria and Switzerland, offering users flexible access to a wide range of fitness experiences across various studios and gyms. By integrating MyClubs with Kenko, your studio can tap into a network of potential clients, increase class attendance, and boost revenue—all while maintaining control over your schedule and pricing.

## Why Integrate with MyClubs?

- **Access to a Targeted Market**: Reach fitness enthusiasts in Austria and Switzerland actively seeking diverse workout options.
- **Fill Empty Spots**: Optimize class capacity by filling vacant spots with MyClubs users.
- **Automated Schedule Sync**: Ensure your class schedule is always up-to-date across platforms.
- **Simplified Billing**: Receive consolidated payments from MyClubs, reducing administrative tasks.
- **Corporate Wellness Opportunities**: Attract corporate clients through MyClubs' partnerships with over 300 companies.

## Setting Up the MyClubs Integration

<Steps>
  <Step title="Apply to Become a MyClubs Partner" icon="user-plus">
    Visit the [MyClubs Partner Page](https://www.myclubs.com/at/en/become-a-partner/) and submit an application to become a partner studio.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Your Studio Profile" icon="user">
    Provide detailed information about your studio, including class types, schedules, and amenities.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect via Kenko Marketplace" icon="plug">
    In your Kenko dashboard, navigate to **Apps → Marketplace**, select **MyClubs**, and follow the prompts to authorize the integration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Class Availability" icon="sliders">
    Choose which classes to list on MyClubs and set capacity limits for MyClubs users.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go Live" icon="tower-cell">
    Once setup is complete, your classes will be available for booking by MyClubs users.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Integration Nuances

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Supported Service Types" icon="list">
    Only group classes are supported for integration with MyClubs. Appointments or one-on-one sessions are not currently eligible.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Client Appearance in Kenko" icon="user">
    MyClubs users who book your classes will automatically be added to your Kenko client list, marked distinctly for easy identification.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Booking and Check-In Process" icon="calendar-check">
    Bookings made through MyClubs will appear in your Kenko schedule. Users must check in via the MyClubs app before attending the class.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Payment Handling" icon="credit-card">
    Payments for MyClubs bookings are processed directly by MyClubs and consolidated into monthly payouts to your studio.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## When to Use This Integration

Integrating with MyClubs is particularly beneficial if

- You're looking to increase class attendance without additional marketing efforts.
- You have underutilized classes that could benefit from increased exposure.
- You aim to attract a diverse clientele seeking varied fitness experiences.
- You want to tap into corporate wellness programs and attract employees from various companies.
