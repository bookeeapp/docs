# Zapier Integration in Kenko

Imagine a world where your apps talk to each other seamlessly, automating tasks and freeing up your time for more important things. With Zapier, that world is a reality. By integrating Kenko with Zapier, you can connect with over 5,000 apps, automating workflows and enhancing productivity without writing a single line of code.

## Why Integrate Zapier?

Zapier acts as a bridge between Kenko and thousands of other applications, enabling you to

- **Automate Repetitive Tasks**: Eliminate manual data entry and routine tasks by setting up automated workflows.
- **Enhance Productivity**: Focus on strategic work by letting Zapier handle the mundane.
- **Connect with Multiple Apps**: Integrate Kenko with CRM systems, email marketing tools, project management apps, and more.
- **Customize Workflows**: Tailor automations to fit your unique business processes.

## Setting Up Zapier Integration

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Zapier Account" icon="user-plus">
    Sign up for a free account at [zapier.com](https://zapier.com/).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Kenko to Zapier" icon="link">
    In Zapier, search for Kenko and connect your account using the provided API key or authentication method.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a Trigger" icon="bolt">
    Select a trigger event in Kenko, such as "New Booking" or "New Lead."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select an Action App" icon="square-bolt">
    Choose the app you want to connect with Kenko, like Mailchimp, Trello, or Google Sheets.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Your Zap" icon="sliders">
    Define the specific details of your workflow, mapping data fields between Kenko and the chosen app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test and Activate" icon="circle-play">
    Test your Zap to ensure it works as expected, then turn it on to start automating your workflow.
  </Step>
</Steps>
