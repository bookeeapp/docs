Get started with the Kenko platform quickly and efficiently. Our structured onboarding process ensures you can make the most of our platform's features to solve your business needs.

## Your Onboarding Journey

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="handshake" title="Initial Consultation">
    After deciding to use Kenko, you'll have a kick-off call with our onboarding team to align on implementation goals, timelines, and success criteria. We'll gather your requirements and create a customized onboarding plan.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="flag-checkered" title="Project Planning">
    Together we'll establish clear milestones, responsibilities, and timelines for your implementation. We'll introduce you to your dedicated onboarding specialist who will guide you through the entire process.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="graduation-cap" title="Training">
    We provide comprehensive training sessions tailored to different user roles within your organization. These sessions cover platform navigation, feature utilization, and best practices to ensure you get maximum value from the Kenko platform.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="gear" title="Account Setup">
    Depending on your business needs, we'll configure your account with either:
    
    - **Branded App Setup**: A custom-branded mobile application for your business
    - **Website Integration**: Seamless integration with your existing website
    
    Our technical team will handle all the necessary configurations to match your branding and specific requirements.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="database" title="Data Migration">
    If you're transitioning from another system, we offer data migration services including:
    
    - **Payment Data**: Securely transfer your existing payment information
    - **Business Data**: Migrate your business-critical information to the Kenko platform
    
    Our migration specialists ensure a smooth transition with minimal disruption to your operations.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="rocket" title="Go-Live">
    Once all preparations are complete, we'll coordinate the launch of your Kenko implementation. Our team will provide close support during this critical phase to address any questions or concerns, ensuring a smooth transition to live operations.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Additional Support

<CardGroup>
  <Card title="Implementation Pause" icon="pause-circle">
    Need to temporarily pause your implementation? No problem. Simply notify your onboarding specialist with:
    
    1. The reason for the pause
    2. Your expected resume date
    
    <Note>
      Please note that subscription charges continue during implementation pauses.
    </Note>
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Need Help?" icon="life-ring">
    At any stage of your onboarding journey, our dedicated support team is ready to assist you. Reach out through your preferred support channel or directly to your onboarding specialist with any questions or concerns.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
