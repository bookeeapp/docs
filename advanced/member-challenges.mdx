# Member Challenges

Member challenges are a powerful tool for increasing engagement, building community, and improving retention at your fitness business. This guide explains how to create and manage effective challenges in the Kenko platform.

## Types of Challenges

Kenko supports various challenge formats to suit different fitness goals:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="calendar-week" title="Attendance Challenges">
    Motivate consistent visits:
    
    - **Class Count**: Complete a target number of classes in a specific timeframe
    - **Visit Streak**: Attend classes for consecutive days/weeks
    - **Class Variety**: Try different class types within a period
    
    **Example**: "20 Classes in 30 Days" or "Try 5 Different Class Types This Month"
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="stopwatch" title="Performance Challenges">
    Focus on measurable achievements:
    
    - **Personal Records**: Beat previous best performances
    - **Progression Tracking**: Improve specific metrics over time
    - **Milestone Achievements**: Reach predefined performance targets
    
    **Example**: "Increase Your Hold Times" or "500 Mile Club"
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="users" title="Team Challenges">
    Create friendly competition:
    
    - **Team vs. Team**: Groups compete against each other
    - **Studio-Wide Goal**: All members work together toward a common target
    - **Partner Challenges**: Members pair up to complete goals
    
    **Example**: "Studio Team Challenge: 1,000 Classes in January" or "Partner Accountability Challenge"
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="trophy" title="Achievement Challenges">
    Recognize member accomplishments:
    
    - **Skills Mastery**: Complete progressively difficult skills
    - **Level-Up System**: Advance through defined achievement levels
    - **Certification Path**: Work toward recognized accomplishments
    
    **Example**: "Yoga Pose Mastery" or "Black Belt Journey"
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Creating a New Challenge

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Challenge Manager">
    Navigate to **Advanced Features** > **Member Challenges** > **Challenge Manager**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start New Challenge">
    Click the **+ New Challenge** button.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Define Challenge Basics">
    Enter the fundamental information:
    - Challenge name
    - Description and purpose
    - Start and end dates
    - Challenge type
    - Visibility settings (public or invitation-only)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Challenge Goals">
    Define what participants need to achieve:
    - Target metrics (classes, minutes, skills, etc.)
    - Completion criteria
    - Progress tracking method
    - Optional milestones/levels
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Teams">
    For team-based challenges:
    - Set team formation method (self-select, random, or assigned)
    - Define team size limits
    - Create team names and designations (or allow member customization)
    
    <Note>
      Skip this step for individual challenges.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Define Rewards">
    Set up incentives for completion:
    - Badges and digital achievements
    - Point rewards
    - Physical prizes
    - Special privileges
    - Recognition methods
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create Communication Plan">
    Configure automated communications:
    - Invitation/registration announcements
    - Progress updates
    - Milestone celebrations
    - Final results and recognition
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Up Leaderboard">
    Configure how progress will be displayed:
    - Metrics shown on leaderboard
    - Refresh frequency
    - Privacy options
    - Display locations (app, studio screens, website)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Publish Challenge">
    Review all details and activate the challenge:
    - Preview how it will appear to members
    - Test tracking functionality
    - Finalize all settings
    - Publish and announce
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Active Challenges

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="user-plus" title="Member Registration">
    Manage challenge participation:
    
    - **Open Enrollment**: Members join through app or member portal
    - **Invitation**: Send targeted invitations to specific member segments
    - **Manual Registration**: Staff add members at check-in or during sessions
    - **Auto-Enrollment**: Include all members meeting certain criteria
    
    Access registration options via **Challenge Manager** > [Challenge Name] > **Participants**.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="chart-bar" title="Tracking Progress">
    Monitor challenge advancement:
    
    - View real-time leaderboards
    - Track individual and team progress
    - Identify members falling behind
    - Send encouragement messages
    - Celebrate milestone achievements
    
    Access tracking tools via **Challenge Manager** > [Challenge Name] > **Progress Tracking**.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="bullhorn" title="Communication">
    Maintain engagement throughout the challenge:
    
    - Send automated progress updates
    - Create mid-challenge motivation messages
    - Highlight outstanding performers
    - Share tips and encouragement
    - Announce changes or updates
    
    Configure communications via **Challenge Manager** > [Challenge Name] > **Communications**.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="cog" title="Adjustments & Modifications">
    Make changes to active challenges when needed:
    
    - Extend or shorten duration
    - Adjust goals or criteria
    - Add bonus activities or opportunities
    - Modify team structures
    - Address any issues or concerns
    
    Access modification tools via **Challenge Manager** > [Challenge Name] > **Settings**.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Promoting Member Challenges

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pre-Launch Promotion">
    Build excitement before the challenge begins:
    - Create teaser announcements
    - Highlight past challenge successes
    - Share testimonials from previous participants
    - Showcase prizes or rewards
    - Post countdown notifications
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Launch Announcement">
    Maximize initial participation:
    - Send dedicated email announcement
    - Create app and in-studio notifications
    - Have instructors mention in classes
    - Post prominent signage
    - Share on social media channels
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Ongoing Engagement">
    Maintain momentum throughout the challenge:
    - Share progress updates and leaderboards
    - Highlight participant stories
    - Post mid-challenge motivation
    - Create milestone celebrations
    - Address any participation gaps
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Conclusion & Recognition">
    Celebrate achievements at challenge end:
    - Announce winners and top participants
    - Recognize all who completed the challenge
    - Share overall challenge statistics
    - Award prizes and recognition
    - Gather feedback for future challenges
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Challenge Analytics & Reporting

After your challenge concludes, analyze its impact:

1. Navigate to **Advanced Features** > **Member Challenges** > **Analytics**
2. Select the completed challenge
3. Review performance metrics:
   - Participation rate
   - Completion percentage
   - Attendance impact
   - Engagement increase
   - Retention correlation
