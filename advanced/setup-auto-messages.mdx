# Setting Up Auto-Messages

Auto-Messages are your studio’s behind-the-scenes workhorse. From appointment reminders to membership expiry alerts, these notifications help keep your clients informed and engaged—automatically.

This guide walks you through the entire setup process and provides recommendations on which messages to send, when, and how.

## Creating a New Auto-Message

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Auto-Messages Panel" icon="wrench">
    Go to **Setup → Notifications** You’ll see a list of all existing notifications, along with their current delivery status and channels.

    ![Screenshot2025 05 13at6 27 16PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-05-13at6.27.16PM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click ‘New Notification’" icon="plus">
    Hit the **New Notification** button at the top right. You’ll enter a step-by-step message builder.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Name, Trigger Group & Event" icon="bolt-lightning">
    Give your message a clear internal name.\
    Choose a **Trigger Group** (e.g., Appointments, Classes, Memberships), and then the exact **Trigger Event** (e.g., Appointment booked).

    ![Screenshot2025 05 13at6 27 29PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-05-13at6.27.29PM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define Recipient & Timing" icon="clock">
    Choose who the message is sent to: **Contact** or **Staff**.\
    Then define when the message should go out (e.g., 30 minutes before, 2 hours after, immediately).

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-05-13at6.27.41PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot2025 05 13at6 27 41PM Pn"
      title="Screenshot2025 05 13at6 27 41PM Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
    />

    | **Recipient** | **Who It Sends To**                 | **Use This For**                                                                          | **Examples**                                                         |
    | ------------- | ----------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------- |
    | **Contact**   | Clients, Members, Leads             | When the message is customer-facing or impacts their experience                           | - Class reminders - Payment failed alerts- Membership renewals       |
    | **Staff**     | Instructors, Admins, Frontdesk Team | When the message is internal or operational (helps your team take action or stay updated) | - Lead assigned to staff - Instructor substitution- Task assignments |

    <Tip>
      If the action is customer-facing, choose **Contact**. If it’s operational, assign it to **Staff**.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Delivery Channel" icon="message">
    Pick one or more channels

    - **Email** — Best for confirmations, follow-ups, and longer content
    - **SMS** — Ideal for reminders and urgent alerts
    - **Push** — Use for short, time-sensitive nudges via the app

    ![Screenshot2025 05 13at6 27 49PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-05-13at6.27.49PM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write the Message" icon="pen">
    Craft your subject and message content. You can use dynamic tags like `{{first_name}}` for personalization.\
    A preview of the message will appear below, and you can send a test to your phone or inbox.

    ![Screenshot2025 05 13at6 28 12PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-05-13at6.28.12PM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set ‘Do Not Deliver’ Window" icon="ban">
    Optionally, prevent messages from going out during certain hours (e.g., 11 PM to 7 AM) to avoid late-night pings.

    ![Screenshot2025 05 13at6 28 50PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-05-13at6.28.50PM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Notification" icon="check">
    Click **Create Notification**. You’re done\! Messages will now trigger based on your setup.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use Clear, Short Language" icon="message">
    Especially for SMS and push messages — keep it short, actionable, and focused. Avoid long paragraphs.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Send Messages at Smart Times" icon="clock">
    Don’t annoy clients with reminders at midnight. Use the “Do Not Deliver” window to control timing.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Personalize With Tags" icon="badge-check">
    Tags like `{{first_name}}`, `{{class_time}}`, and `{{instructor}}` make your messages feel human.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Test Before You Launch" icon="flask">
    Always send yourself a test email/SMS to preview formatting and timing before activating a message.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Audit Your Flows Quarterly" icon="clipboard-list">
    Review which messages are working (or not). Remove outdated ones and optimize copy based on engagement.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
