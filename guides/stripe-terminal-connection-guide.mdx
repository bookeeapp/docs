## How to Register and Set Up Your Terminal

<Steps>
  <Step title="Register your business location with Stripe">
    Go to your [Stripe Dashboard](https://dashboard.stripe.com/register) and make sure your **location in Stripe** matches your **Kenko CRM location**.

    <Info>
      If your Stripe location doesn't match the CRM, the connection will fail.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Reader Registration in Stripe">
    From your Stripe dashboard, go to Terminal â†’ Locations and click Register Reader.
    Give your location a name that matches the one in Kenko.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Power up the Stripe Reader">
    Turn on your reader and navigate to the screen where it shows a pairing code.
    The format should look like: <code>
      WSC-###
    </code>
     or <code>
      CSR-###
    </code>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the pairing code in Stripe">
    In your Stripe dashboard, input the pairing code under the "Register Reader" modal and assign the device to a location.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Success! Device now shows in your dashboard">
    Once registered, you'll see your reader listed and ready to connect to Kenko.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Linking Stripe Reader with Kenko

Now that your terminal is registered, it's time to connect it in Kenko.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Head to Settings > Stripe > Card Readers">
    You'll see a list of registered readers from Stripe.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Connect' on your desired reader">
    Select the reader you'd like to connect to your POS.
  </Step>
  <Step title="That's it â€“ you're good to go!">
    Once connected, you can use the terminal for POS payments.
    No need to reconnect unless you reset the terminal.
  </Step>
</Steps>
