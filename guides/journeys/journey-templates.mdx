# Journey Templates

Kenko offers a library of professionally designed journey templates to help you quickly implement effective member experiences. These templates are fully customizable and cover a range of business types and member goals.

## Using Journey Templates

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Template Library">
    1. Navigate to **Marketing > Journeys**
    2. Click the **Create New Journey** button
    3. Select the **Start from Template** option
    4. Browse categories or use the search function to find relevant templates
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Preview and Select">
    1. Click on any template to see:
       - Template overview and intended outcomes
       - Stage breakdown with descriptions
       - Estimated completion time
       - Member feedback and success metrics from other businesses
    2. Use the **Preview as Member** option to experience the journey flow
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Customize Template">
    1. Click **Use This Template** to create a copy
    2. The template will open in edit mode
    3. Customize each element to align with your brand and specific objectives
    4. Update content, requirements, and resources to match your offerings
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Implement and Launch">
    Follow the standard journey launch process:
    1. Preview and test the customized journey
    2. Set enrollment options
    3. Publish and promote to your members
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Building Your Own Templates

Once you've created successful journeys, you can save them as templates for future use:

1. Go to **Marketing > Journeys** and select the journey you want to templatize
2. Click the **Save as Template** button
3. Complete the template details:
   - Name and description
   - Category and tags for easy finding
   - Notes on best practices and results
4. Choose sharing settings:
   - Private (your business only)
   - Multi-location (across your locations)
   - Community (share with other Kenko users)

<Note>
If you share templates to the community and they become featured in the template library, you may be eligible for Kenko credit rewards as part of our knowledge-sharing program.
</Note>
