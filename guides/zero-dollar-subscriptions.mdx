# \$0 Subscription Plan in Kenko

The **\$0 Subscription Plan** helps you retain more users, reduce drop-offs during the checkout process, and support users who subscribe through insurance or corporate wellness programs.

<Info>
  Let customers subscribe without needing to enter payment information—ideal for partnerships, trials, and conversion campaigns.
</Info>

## Why Offer a \$0 Plan?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Attract New Customers" icon="users">
    Offering a free plan lets new customers try out your services without commitment, increasing the likelihood they’ll convert to paid members later.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Reduce Drop-Offs at Checkout" icon="bag-shopping">
    Customers often abandon the purchase journey when payment is required upfront. A \$0 plan eliminates that friction.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Support Sponsored Memberships" icon="check">
    Many customers are eligible for corporate wellness or insurance-funded memberships. A \$0 plan lets them subscribe without out-of-pocket expenses.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How to Set Up a \$0 Subscription Plan

<Card>
  To create a \$0 subscription

  - Go to your admin panel → **Pricing Options**
  - Start a new subscription plan
  - In the **Price** field, enter `0`
  - Toggle auto-renewal on/off as needed
</Card>

<info>
  See the full guide: [Creating a subscription plan](https://help.bookeeapp.com/en/articles/5373750-creating-a-subscription-plan)
</info>

## Where Can Customers Purchase This?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="From the Website or Customer App">
    Customers can buy \$0 subscriptions directly from your studio’s website or the Kenko customer mobile app.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="From the CRM or Business App">
    Studio staff can assign \$0 plans via the Kenko CRM or Business App when onboarding or assisting customers.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<info>
  Learn how to sell a subscription: [How to sell a subscription plan](https://help.bookeeapp.com/en/articles/5381458-how-to-sell-a-subscription-plan-membership-plan)
</info>

## What Happens After Purchase?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="No Payment Method Collected" icon="credit-card">
    Customers purchasing \$0 plans will not be required to enter card details or choose a payment method.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Transaction Recorded as Cash" icon="money-bill">
    In your system, these transactions appear as **cash payments**, even though no monetary exchange takes place.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Example Use Case

<Card>
  You’re running a partnership with a local insurance provider. Eligible customers are allowed one free membership per year.

  - Create a \$0 subscription plan named “Insurance Partner Plan”
  - Share a direct link or let staff assign it via CRM
  - Customers subscribe without any payment entry
</Card>
