# Quickstart

Welcome to Kenko! Follow these simple steps to get started with the platform. This guide will help you set up your account and start using the key features to streamline your business.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin setting up your Kenko account, make sure you have the following information ready

\- Business name, address, Payment information and other necessary details. - Logo and brand colors for customization of the website widget Intro offers for new leads, Memberships and Credit packs for your customers. Set up Classes, Appointments or Courses as offerings for your customers

## Setup Process

Your setup starts with exisiting data migration if You are coming from some other software, Once all the data has been migrated Your account is ready for further setup. Set up your business information and location details to the CRM.



**Region & Location:**

Set up regions (e.g., New York, Los Angeles) and locations (e.g., Downtown Studio, Manhattan Studio) for better business management.

|                | Region                                                | Location                                                     |
| -------------- | ----------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Definition** | A broader geographic or organizational area           | A specific physical facility or business unit                |
| **Scope**      | Can contain multiple locations                        | Belongs to a single region                                   |
| **Usecase**    | Organize operations across cities or states           | Manage services and staff at a specific site                 |
| **Timezone**   | All locations in a region will have the same Timezone | A location's Timezone is dependent on it's region's Timezone |

Add staff members ( Instructors, Operators, Front desk staff ) to the CRM and then invite them via email.

Assigning roles and Permissions to each member which their accessibility in the CRM.
