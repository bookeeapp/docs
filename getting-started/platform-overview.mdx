# Kenko Platform Overview

Kenko is designed as an integrated platform with multiple modules that work together to provide a comprehensive solution for fitness and wellness businesses. This overview will help you understand how the different parts of the platform connect and interact.

## Module Interactions

Understanding how these modules work together helps you maximize the platform's value:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Member Journey">
    A new client discovers your business, books a class through your **Website & App**, becomes a member through **Member Management**, and starts paying through **Billing & Payments**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Operational Flow">
    You set up classes in **Scheduling**, members book them through the mobile app, check in is recorded in **Member Management**, and attendance data flows to **Reports & Analytics**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Business Growth">
    **Analytics** identify potential for a new service, you promote it via **Marketing Tools**, add it to your schedule in **Scheduling**, and track its financial performance through **Billing & Payments**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Role-Based Access

Kenko uses role-based permissions to ensure each team member has appropriate access:

| Role | Primary Access | Capabilities |
| ---- | -------------- | ------------ |
| Owner/Admin | Full System | Complete access to all features and settings |
| Manager | Operations | Member management, scheduling, reports, limited settings |
| Front Desk | Member Interaction | Check-in, bookings, basic member info, point of sale |
| Instructor | Class Management | Class rosters, attendance, member notes |
| Billing Admin | Financial | Payments, invoices, financial reports |
| Marketing Admin | Promotion | Campaigns, referrals, communications |
