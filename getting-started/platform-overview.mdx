## Module Interactions

Kenko’s modules are designed to work together seamlessly, optimizing the efficiency of your business operations. Here’s how they interact:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Customer Journey">
    A new client discovers your business via your **Website & App**, books a class, becomes a member through **Contact Management**, and starts paying via **Billing & Payments**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Operational Flow">
    You set up your classes in **Scheduling**, members book through the mobile app, check-ins are recorded in **Contact Management**, and attendance data flows to **Reports & Analytics** for insights.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Business Growth">
    **Reports & Analytics** reveal opportunities for new services. You promote these through **Marketing Tools**, add them to your schedule with **Scheduling**, and track the financial impact through **Billing & Payments**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Role-Based Access

Kenko uses role-based access control to ensure that each team member has the appropriate permissions.

You can define a role and decide which permission module you want them to have access to.

| Permission Category | Permission module              | Description                                                                                                          | Can Be Toggled On/Off |
| ------------------- | ------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------- |
| **Contacts & POS**  | Contacts & POS                 | Allows access to customer data and the ability to sell products, services, and courses through CRM and Business app. | Yes                   |
| **Operations**      | Calendar                       | Allows access to and modification of the calendar. Without contacts permission, users cannot book customers.         | Yes                   |
|                     | Tasks                          | Users can add and edit tasks within the platform.                                                                    | Yes                   |
|                     | Marketing                      | Allows users to create, run, and edit marketing campaigns.                                                           | Yes                   |
|                     | Reports                        | Users can view and download business reports.                                                                        | Yes                   |
| **Admin**           | Settings                       | Users can access and modify general settings of the business, including design and communication settings.           | Yes                   |
|                     | Setup                          | Users can manage business setup, including services, products, discounts, and notifications.                         | Yes                   |
|                     | Add New Staff                  | Ability to add and edit staff profiles.                                                                              | Yes                   |
|                     | Staff Attendance               | Manage staff attendance and clock in/out functionality.                                                              | Yes                   |
|                     | Roles and Privileges           | Ability to add or modify roles and privileges for other staff members.                                               | Yes                   |
|                     | Account & Billing              | Users can access account settings, modify subscription, and purchase add-ons like SMS packs.                         | Yes                   |
| **Inbox**           | Inbox                          | Users can access the inbox and send messages from Bookee.                                                            | Yes                   |
| **Website Builder** | Website Builder                | Access to the website builder to create and customize the website.                                                   | Yes                   |
| **Business App**    | Add & Edit Events              | Allows users to add and edit events within the business app.                                                         | Yes                   |
|                     | Show Events of All Instructors | Ability to view events from all instructors in the app.                                                              | Yes                   |
|                     | Show Contact Details           | Users can view email and phone number details of contacts in the app.                                                | Yes                   |

## Best Practices for Role-Based Permissions

| **Role**            | **Primary Access (Modules)**                       | **Capabilities**                                               | **Best Practices**                                                                                                       |
| ------------------- | -------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Owner/Admin**     | Full System                                        | Complete access to all features and settings                   | Owner gets access to all modules by default.                                                                             |
| **Manager**         | Contact Management, Scheduling, Reports, Marketing | Contact query Resolving, scheduling, reports, limited settings | Assign to managerial roles who need to oversee day-to-day operations but restrict access to sensitive settings.          |
| **Front Desk**      | Contact Management, Scheduling, Billing & Payments | Check-ins, bookings, basic member info, point of sale          | Perfect for front desk staff who interact directly with members and manage bookings or payments.                         |
| **Instructor**      | Scheduling, Contact Management                     | Class rosters, attendance, member notes                        | Suitable for instructors who need access to class schedules and student records but not financial or marketing settings. |
| **Billing Admin**   | Billing & Payments, Reports                        | Payments, invoices, financial reports                          | Ideal for handling payments and financial reporting. Restrict access to sensitive business settings and member data.     |
| **Marketing Admin** | Marketing, Reports                                 | Campaigns, referrals, communications                           | Best for marketing staff who need access to create and manage campaigns but should not handle financial or member data.  |
