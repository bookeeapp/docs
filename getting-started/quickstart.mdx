# Kenko Quickstart Guide

Welcome to Kenko\! Follow these simple steps to get started with the platform. This guide will help you set up your account and start using the key features to streamline your business.

## Setup Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Data Migration and Account Setup">
    Your setup starts with exisiting data migration if You are coming from some other software, Once all the data has been migrated Your account is ready for further setup.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Business Profile Setup">
    Set up your business information and location details to the CRM.

    <Note>
      **Region & Location**: Set up regions (e.g., New York, Los Angeles) and locations (e.g., Downtown Studio, Manhattan Studio) for better business management.

      |            | Region                                                | Location                                                     |
      | ---------- | ----------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
      | Definition | A broader geographic or organizational area           | A specific physical facility or business unit                |
      | Scope      | Can contain multiple locations                        | Belongs to a single region                                   |
      | Usecase    | Organize operations across cities or states           | Manage services and staff at a specific site                 |
      | Timezone   | All locations in a region will have the same Timezone | A location's Timezone is dependent on it's region's Timezone |
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="User Roles & Permissions">
    Add staff members ( Instructors, Operators, Front desk staff ) to the CRM and then invite them via email.

    <Info>
      Assigning roles and Permissions to each member which  their accessibility in the CRM.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Services & Pricing">
    Set up your business offerings ( Pricing options, Classes, Appointments, Courses, Videos, Merchandise, Gift cards etc.)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Payment Setup">
    Configure your payment settings by connecting it with integrated Payment getways inside the Kenko CRM ( Stripe, Adyen etc)

    <Note>
      Do not forget to Add tax rates, Invoice information and Payment policies
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch Your Portal">
    Customize your website widget and embed it into your website and you are ready to LaunchðŸš€

    <Tip>
      Don't have a website? Contact [support@gokenko.com](mailto:support@gokenko.com) to get your hands on the best website for your business.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Further steps">
    Once your website is live, You can download the Kenko app and get access to your own branded app for both your business and customers\!.
  </Step>
</Steps>
