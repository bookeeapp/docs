# Kenko Quickstart Guide

Get up and running with the Kenko platform in just a few steps. This guide will help you set up your account and start using the core features.

## Before You Begin

Before setting up your Kenko account, gather the following information:

- Business details (name, address, contact information)
- Logo and brand colors (for customization)
- Staff information (for user accounts)
- Membership/pricing structure
- Class schedule (if applicable)

## Setup Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Sign up at [app.gokenko.com](https://app.gokenko.com) with your business email. Verify your email address and set a secure password.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Business Profile">
    Enter your business details including:
    - Business name and type
    - Location information
    - Contact details
    - Business hours
    - Upload your logo
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Up User Access">
    Add staff members who need access to the platform:
    - Invite staff via email
    - Assign appropriate roles and permissions
    - Set up staff profiles and schedules
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Offerings">
    Set up your services, classes, and membership options:
    - Create membership plans and pricing
    - Add classes and services
    - Set capacities and durations
    - Configure availability
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Payment Settings">
    Connect your payment processor:
    - Set up Stripe or other payment integration
    - Configure tax settings
    - Set up payment policies
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Launch Your Portal">
    Make your business available to members:
    - Customize your member portal
    - Set up your booking widget
    - Configure notification settings
    - Test the member experience
  </Step>
</Steps>
