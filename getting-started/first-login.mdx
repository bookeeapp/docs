# Your First Login

After creating your Kenko account, your first login experience will guide you through essential setup steps and introduce you to the platform. This guide walks you through what to expect and how to navigate the system.

## Accessing Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Login Page">
    Go to [app.gokenko.com](https://app.gokenko.com) in your web browser.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Credentials">
    Input the email address and password you used during signup.
    
    <Note>
      If you received an invitation email, use the "Set Password" link from that email before your first login.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Two-Factor Authentication">
    If enabled, you'll be prompted to enter a verification code sent to your mobile device or email.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Accept Terms">
    Review and accept the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy if prompted.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## First-Time Setup Wizard

On your first login, you'll be guided through our setup wizard:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="building" title="Business Profile" defaultOpen>
    Complete key details about your business:
    - Legal business name
    - Business type
    - Contact information
    - Location details
    - Logo upload
    - Brand color selection
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="credit-card" title="Payment Setup">
    Connect your payment processing:
    - Select your payment provider
    - Set up account connection
    - Configure basic payment settings
    - Add tax information
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="tags" title="Service Configuration">
    Define your basic service offerings:
    - Add membership types
    - Set pricing options
    - Define class types
    - Create basic schedule
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="users-cog" title="Staff Accounts">
    Invite key team members:
    - Add staff email addresses
    - Assign appropriate roles
    - Set access permissions
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Note>
  You can skip certain sections of the wizard and return to them later, but completing the Business Profile is required.
</Note>

### Navigation Menu

The main navigation menu is located on the left side of the screen and provides access to all platform modules:

| Menu Item | Function |
| --------- | -------- |
| Dashboard | Return to the main overview screen |
| Members | Access member profiles and management tools |
| Schedule | View and manage your class and appointment calendar |
| Billing | Handle payments, invoices, and financial settings |
| Marketing | Access promotion and communication tools |
| Reports | View business analytics and generate reports |
| Settings | Configure your account and system preferences |

## Personalization Options

Take a moment to personalize your experience:

1. **User Profile**: Click your profile picture in the top-right corner to:
   - Update your personal information
   - Change your password
   - Set notification preferences
   - Configure display settings

2. **Favorites**: Star frequently used pages to add them to your Favorites bar for quick access

3. **Dashboard Widgets**: Customize your dashboard by:
   - Clicking "Customize Dashboard" in the top-right of the dashboard
   - Dragging and reordering widgets
   - Adding or removing information cards
