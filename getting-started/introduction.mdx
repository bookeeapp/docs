# Welcome to Kenko

Kenko is an all-in-one platform designed specifically for fitness and wellness businesses. Whether you run a yoga studio, fitness center, or wellness spa, Kenko provides the tools you need to manage and grow your business.

## What is Kenko?

Kenko combines member management, scheduling, billing, marketing, and analytics into a single integrated platform that helps you:

- **Streamline Operations**: Simplify day-to-day tasks and reduce administrative burden
- **Enhance Member Experience**: Create seamless booking and communication processes
- **Boost Revenue**: Optimize pricing, increase retention, and find new growth opportunities
- **Make Data-Driven Decisions**: Access insights that help you improve your business
